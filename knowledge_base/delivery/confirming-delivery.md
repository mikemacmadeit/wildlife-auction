# Confirming Delivery

Confirming delivery is an important step in the purchase process.

## When to Confirm

- **After receiving the item** and inspecting it
- **Item matches listing** description
- **No major issues** or damage
- **Within protection window**

## How to Confirm

1. **Go to "My Orders"**
2. **Find your order**
3. **Click "Confirm Delivery"**
4. **Review and confirm**

## What Happens After Confirming

- **Protection window starts** - You have time to inspect
- **Seller can request payout** (for protected transactions)
- **Order status updates** to "Delivered"
- **You can still report issues** during protection window

## Before Confirming

**Inspect the item:**
- Check it matches the listing description
- Verify condition matches photos
- Test functionality if applicable
- Check for damage or missing parts

**If there are issues:**
- **Don't confirm yet** - Contact seller first
- **Document issues** with photos
- **Try to resolve** with seller
- **Open dispute** if needed

## Protection Window

- **Time to inspect** after delivery confirmation
- **Report issues** during this window
- **Length varies** by transaction type
- **Use this time** to thoroughly check your purchase

## Important Notes

- **Confirming delivery** doesn't mean you can't report issues
- **Protection window** gives you time to inspect
- **Contact seller first** if you have concerns
- **Open dispute** if seller doesn't resolve issues

## Troubleshooting

**Can't confirm delivery?**
- Make sure you've received the item
- Check order status
- Contact support if needed
