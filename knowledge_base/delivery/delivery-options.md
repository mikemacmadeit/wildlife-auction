# Delivery and Shipping Options

Understanding delivery options and how they work on Agchange.

## Delivery Methods

### Seller-Provided Delivery
- **Seller arranges** transportation
- **Seller sets terms** and costs
- **Discuss details** with seller before purchase
- **Common for livestock** and large items

### Buyer Pickup
- **Buyer collects** item from seller location
- **No shipping costs**
- **Arrange time** with seller
- **Bring identification** and payment confirmation

### Third-Party Transport
- **Buyer arranges** their own transport
- **Seller prepares** item for pickup
- **Coordinate timing** with seller
- **Common for specialized items**

## Before Purchase

- **Check listing** for delivery information
- **Contact seller** to discuss options
- **Confirm costs** and timing
- **Ask questions** about delivery process

## Delivery Costs

- **Vary by method** and distance
- **Seller may include** in listing price
- **May be separate** charge
- **Discuss with seller** before purchase

## Delivery Timeline

- **Varies by method** and location
- **Seller will communicate** expected timeline
- **Track in order page** for updates
- **Contact seller** if delayed

## Important Notes

- **Delivery is seller's responsibility** unless otherwise agreed
- **Coordinate details** through order messages
- **Confirm delivery** when you receive item
- **Protection window** starts after delivery confirmation

## For Sellers

- **Specify delivery options** in your listing
- **Communicate clearly** with buyers
- **Provide updates** on delivery status
- **Confirm delivery** when complete
