# Tracking Your Delivery

How to track your order and know when it will arrive.

## Tracking Information

### Where to Find It
- **Order page** - Go to "My Orders" and click on your order
- **Email notifications** - Check for tracking updates
- **Seller messages** - Seller may share tracking info
- **Order status** - Status updates show delivery progress

### What You'll See
- **Tracking number** - If seller provides one
- **Carrier information** - Shipping company name
- **Estimated delivery** - Expected arrival date
- **Current status** - Where item is in transit

## Delivery Status Updates

### Order Status
- **Paid** - Payment received, seller preparing
- **In Transit** - Item has been shipped
- **Delivered** - Item has arrived (you confirm)
- **Accepted** - You've accepted the item

### Tracking Status
- **Label created** - Shipping label generated
- **In transit** - Item is on the way
- **Out for delivery** - Being delivered today
- **Delivered** - Arrived at destination

## If Tracking Not Available

### Seller-Provided Delivery
- **No tracking** - May not have tracking number
- **Contact seller** - Ask for delivery updates
- **Check order page** - Status updates there
- **Be patient** - Seller will contact you

### Buyer Pickup
- **No tracking** - You're picking up directly
- **Coordinate with seller** - Arrange pickup time
- **Confirm location** - Verify pickup address
- **Bring ID** - May need to show identification

## What to Do

### While Waiting
- **Check order page** - Regular status updates
- **Check email** - Delivery notifications
- **Contact seller** - If you have questions
- **Be available** - For delivery or pickup

### If Delayed
- **Check tracking** - See current status
- **Contact seller** - Ask about delay
- **Check weather** - May affect delivery
- **Contact carrier** - If using shipping service

### When It Arrives
- **Inspect immediately** - Check item condition
- **Confirm delivery** - Mark as received on platform
- **Report issues** - If item damaged or wrong
- **Leave feedback** - Optional review

## Tips

- **Save tracking number** - Keep it handy
- **Check regularly** - But don't obsess
- **Be patient** - Delivery takes time
- **Contact support** - If significantly delayed
