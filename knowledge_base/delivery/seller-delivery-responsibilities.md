# Seller Delivery Responsibilities

What sellers are responsible for when it comes to delivery.

## Your Responsibilities

- **Arrange delivery** - Coordinate with buyer
- **Prepare item** - Item ready for pickup/delivery
- **Communicate** - Keep buyer informed of status
- **Deliver as described** - Item matches listing
- **Handle issues** - Resolve delivery problems

## Delivery Options

### Seller-Provided Delivery
- **You arrange** transportation
- **You set terms** and costs
- **You coordinate** timing with buyer
- **You're responsible** for safe delivery

### Buyer Pickup
- **You prepare** item for pickup
- **You coordinate** pickup time
- **You verify** buyer identity
- **You hand over** item safely

### Third-Party Transport
- **Buyer arranges** their own transport
- **You prepare** item for pickup
- **You coordinate** with buyer's transporter
- **You verify** pickup completion

## Communication

- **Respond promptly** - Answer buyer questions quickly
- **Provide updates** - Keep buyer informed of status
- **Share tracking** - If using shipping service
- **Confirm delivery** - Verify when item is delivered

## Best Practices

- **Be clear in listing** - Specify delivery options upfront
- **Respond quickly** - Don't leave buyers waiting
- **Pack carefully** - Ensure item arrives safely
- **Document delivery** - Keep records of delivery confirmation
- **Handle problems** - Address issues promptly

## Common Issues

**Buyer not available:**
- Reschedule delivery
- Communicate clearly
- Document attempts

**Item damaged:**
- Work with buyer to resolve
- Consider partial refund if appropriate
- Contact support if needed

**Delivery delayed:**
- Communicate with buyer
- Provide updates
- Apologize for inconvenience

## Important Notes

- **Delivery is your responsibility** unless otherwise agreed
- **Buyer protection** applies - Handle issues fairly
- **Disputes possible** - Resolve issues before they escalate
- **Good communication** prevents most problems
