# How Long Does Delivery Take?

Delivery times vary based on the item, location, and delivery method. Here's what to expect.

## Delivery Timeline Overview

**Typical delivery times:**
- **Local pickup:** Same day to 3 days (arranged with seller)
- **Local delivery:** 1-5 days (arranged with seller)
- **Animal transport:** 3-14 days (depends on distance and transport company)
- **Equipment shipping:** 5-14 business days (depends on shipping method)

## Factors Affecting Delivery Time

### 1. Item Type

**Live Animals:**
- Requires specialized transport
- May need health certificates
- Transport companies schedule pickups
- Typically 3-14 days depending on distance

**Equipment/Vehicles:**
- Can use standard shipping or transport
- May require special handling
- Typically 5-14 business days

**Small Items:**
- Can use standard shipping
- Typically 3-7 business days

### 2. Distance

**Local (same state/region):**
- Fastest delivery
- 1-5 days typically
- Often can arrange pickup

**Regional (neighboring states):**
- 3-7 days typically
- May use transport company

**Long distance (cross-country):**
- 7-14 days typically
- Requires transport company
- May have longer wait times

### 3. Delivery Method

**Pickup:**
- Fastest option
- Arranged directly with seller
- Same day to 3 days typically
- You go to seller's location

**Seller Delivery:**
- Arranged with seller
- 1-5 days typically
- Depends on seller's schedule
- May have delivery fees

**Transport Company:**
- Professional animal transport
- 3-14 days typically
- Scheduled by transport company
- More reliable for long distances

**Standard Shipping:**
- For equipment/small items
- 5-14 business days
- Depends on shipping method chosen

## What Happens After Purchase

**Immediate (Day 1):**
- Order is confirmed
- Payment is processed
- Seller is notified
- You receive order confirmation email

**Within 24-48 Hours:**
- Seller should contact you
- Delivery/pickup arrangements discussed
- Transport scheduled (if needed)
- You'll receive updates

**Delivery Window:**
- Delivery/pickup happens
- You inspect the item
- Confirm delivery in your order
- Funds released to seller

## Communicating with Seller

**Important:**
- **Seller should contact you** within 24-48 hours
- **Discuss delivery options** - Pickup, seller delivery, or transport
- **Agree on timeline** - Set expectations for delivery
- **Stay in communication** - Keep seller updated on your availability

**If seller doesn't contact you:**
- Wait 48 hours (sellers may be busy)
- Send a message through the order page
- Contact support if no response after 3 days

## Delivery Options Explained

### Option 1: Local Pickup

**How it works:**
- You go to seller's location
- Pick up the item in person
- Inspect before taking
- Confirm delivery in order

**Timeline:**
- Arranged within 1-3 days typically
- Depends on your and seller's schedules
- Fastest delivery option

**What to bring:**
- Valid ID
- Payment confirmation (if needed)
- Transportation for the item
- Any required permits or documentation

### Option 2: Seller Delivery

**How it works:**
- Seller brings item to you
- Arranged location and time
- Inspect upon delivery
- Confirm delivery in order

**Timeline:**
- 1-5 days typically
- Depends on seller's schedule and distance
- May have delivery fees

**What to expect:**
- Seller will contact you to arrange
- Agree on delivery location and time
- Be available for delivery
- Inspect item before confirming delivery

### Option 3: Professional Transport

**How it works:**
- Professional transport company handles delivery
- Scheduled pickup from seller
- Transported to your location
- You receive notification when delivered

**Timeline:**
- 3-14 days typically
- Depends on distance and transport company schedule
- More reliable for long distances

**What to expect:**
- Transport company will contact you
- Schedule pickup and delivery dates
- Receive tracking information
- Inspect item upon delivery

## Tracking Your Order

**How to track:**
1. Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard
2. Click on your order
3. View order status and updates
4. Check for messages from seller
5. See delivery timeline

**Order statuses:**
- **Payment Pending** - Waiting for payment to clear (ACH/wire)
- **Processing** - Payment cleared, seller preparing
- **In Transit** - Item is being delivered
- **Delivered** - Item has been delivered (you confirm)
- **Completed** - Order is complete, funds released

## What If Delivery Is Delayed?

**If seller hasn't contacted you:**
- Wait 48 hours (sellers may be busy)
- Send a polite message through the order
- Contact support if no response after 3 days

**If delivery is taking longer than expected:**
- Communicate with seller about timeline
- Check if there are delays (weather, transport issues)
- Be patient but stay in communication
- Contact support if delivery is significantly delayed

**If item doesn't arrive:**
- Contact seller first
- Check order status and tracking
- Contact support if seller is unresponsive
- Open a dispute if item never arrives

## Tips for Faster Delivery

**For buyers:**
- **Respond quickly** to seller messages
- **Be flexible** with pickup/delivery times
- **Have transportation ready** if picking up
- **Be available** when delivery is scheduled

**For sellers:**
- **Contact buyer within 24 hours** of purchase
- **Be responsive** to buyer questions
- **Schedule delivery promptly** when possible
- **Keep buyer updated** on delivery status
