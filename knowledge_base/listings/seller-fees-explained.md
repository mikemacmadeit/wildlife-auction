# What Are Seller Fees?

Understanding the fees associated with selling on Agchange.

## Overview

**Agchange fees:**
- **Listing fees:** Typically free or low cost to list
- **Transaction fees:** Percentage of final sale price
- **Payment processing:** Handled by payment processor (Stripe)
- **No hidden fees:** All fees are transparent

## Fee Structure

### Listing Fees

**Most listings:**
- **Free to list** - No upfront cost to create a listing
- **No listing fees** for most categories
- **Publish when ready** - No charge until item sells

**Premium features (if available):**
- Featured listings may have optional fees
- Promoted listings may have optional fees
- Check current pricing in your seller dashboard

### Transaction Fees

**When fees apply:**
- Fees are charged only when your item sells
- Calculated as a percentage of the final sale price
- Deducted automatically from your payout
- You receive the remaining amount

**Fee percentage:**
- Varies by category and listing type
- Typically 5-10% of final sale price
- Check current rates in your seller dashboard
- Fees are clearly shown before you publish

**Example:**
- Item sells for $1,000
- Transaction fee: 8% = $80
- You receive: $920 (minus payment processing fees)

### Payment Processing Fees

**Stripe fees:**
- Payment processor (Stripe) charges processing fees
- Typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Deducted from your payout
- Standard for online payment processing

**Example:**
- Sale price: $1,000
- Transaction fee (8%): $80
- Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30): $29.30
- You receive: $890.70

## When Fees Are Charged

**Listing creation:**
- **No fee** when you create a listing
- **No fee** if listing doesn't sell
- **No fee** if you end listing early

**When item sells:**
- Transaction fee deducted from sale price
- Payment processing fee deducted
- Remaining amount sent to your payment account
- Fees are automatic and transparent

## Fee Transparency

**You'll see fees:**
- **Before publishing** - Estimated fees shown
- **In listing dashboard** - Current fee structure
- **At sale time** - Exact fees calculated
- **In payout** - Clear breakdown of all fees

**No surprises:**
- All fees are disclosed upfront
- You know exactly what you'll receive
- Fees are standard and competitive
- No hidden charges

## Reducing Fees

**Tips to maximize your payout:**
- **Price competitively** - Higher prices = higher fees, but more profit
- **Complete your profile** - Verified sellers may get better rates
- **Build your reputation** - Good sellers may qualify for lower fees
- **Sell quality items** - Better items sell faster and for more

## Fee Comparison

**Agchange vs. other platforms:**
- Competitive fee structure
- No listing fees (unlike some platforms)
- Fees only when you sell
- Transparent pricing

## Understanding Your Payout

**What you'll receive:**
- Sale price
- Minus transaction fee
- Minus payment processing fee
- Equals your payout

**Payout timeline:**
- Funds held until buyer confirms delivery
- Released to you after delivery confirmation
- Processed to your payment account
- Typically 2-5 business days after release

## Fee Questions

**Common questions:**
- **"Do I pay if my item doesn't sell?"** - No, fees only apply when item sells
- **"Can I see fees before listing?"** - Yes, fees are shown in your dashboard
- **"Are fees negotiable?"** - Fees are standard for all sellers
- **"When do I pay fees?"** - Fees are deducted automatically when item sells
