# Creating a Listing

Learn how to create and publish listings on Agchange.

## Before You Start

- Complete your seller profile
- Have high-quality photos ready
- Know your pricing strategy
- Understand listing requirements for your category

## Step-by-Step Process

### 1. Choose Listing Type

- **Auction**: Bidders compete, highest bid wins
- **Fixed Price**: Set a price, buyer pays immediately

### 2. Select Category

Choose the appropriate category:
- Registered & Specialty Livestock
- Cattle & Livestock
- Horses & Equestrian
- Ranch Equipment
- And more...

### 3. Add Details

- **Title**: Clear, descriptive title
- **Description**: Detailed information about the item
- **Photos**: Upload at least one high-quality photo
- **Location**: Where the item is located

### 4. Set Pricing

**For Auctions:**
- Starting bid amount
- Reserve price (optional)
- Auction duration (1-10 days)

**For Fixed Price:**
- Fixed price amount
- Best Offer option (optional)

### 5. Add Attributes

Fill in category-specific attributes:
- Species, breed, age, health status
- Equipment specifications
- Any other relevant details

### 6. Review and Publish

- Review all information for accuracy
- Check photos are clear and accurate
- Publish your listing

## Listing Best Practices

- Use clear, descriptive titles
- Include multiple high-quality photos
- Write detailed, honest descriptions
- Set competitive pricing
- Respond promptly to buyer questions

## After Publishing

- Your listing appears in search results
- Buyers can contact you with questions
- You'll receive notifications for activity
- Manage your listing from the Seller Dashboard
