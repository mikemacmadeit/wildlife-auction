# Best Offer Feature

The Best Offer feature allows buyers to make offers below the asking price on fixed-price listings.

## For Sellers

### Enabling Best Offer

- **Available on fixed-price listings** only
- **Optional feature** - Enable when creating listing
- **Set minimum price** - Lowest offer you'll accept
- **Set auto-accept price** - Automatically accept offers at or above this price

### Managing Offers

- **Receive offers** - Get notified when offers come in
- **Review offers** - See offer amount and buyer info
- **Accept, counter, or decline** - Your choice for each offer
- **Auto-accept** - Offers at auto-accept price are accepted automatically

### Offer Settings

- **Allow counter-offers** - Buyers can respond to your counter
- **Offer expiry** - Offers expire after set time (default 48 hours)
- **Minimum price** - Set your lowest acceptable price

## For Buyers

### Making an Offer

1. **Find fixed-price listing** with Best Offer enabled
2. **Click "Make Offer"**
3. **Enter your offer amount**
4. **Submit offer**
5. **Wait for seller response**

### After Making Offer

- **Seller reviews** your offer
- **Seller can accept, counter, or decline**
- **You'll be notified** of the response
- **If accepted** - Complete checkout immediately
- **If countered** - You can accept, counter again, or decline

### Offer Tips

- **Research value** - Know what the item is worth
- **Start reasonable** - Don't lowball too much
- **Be ready to buy** - Offers are binding if accepted
- **Respond promptly** - Offers expire after set time

## Best Practices

**For Sellers:**
- Set realistic minimum price
- Respond to offers promptly
- Consider all offers fairly
- Use auto-accept for quick sales

**For Buyers:**
- Make reasonable offers
- Be ready to complete purchase
- Respond to counters quickly
- Don't make offers you won't honor
