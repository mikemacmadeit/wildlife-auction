# Prohibited Items and Content

Agchange has rules about what can and cannot be listed or sold.

## Prohibited Items

- **Illegal wildlife** - Items that violate wildlife laws
- **Endangered species** - Protected species without proper permits
- **Stolen items** - Any stolen property
- **Counterfeit items** - Fake or replica items
- **Hazardous materials** - Dangerous or toxic substances
- **Weapons** - Firearms and certain weapons (check local laws)

## Prohibited Content

- **Misleading information** - False or deceptive descriptions
- **Inappropriate content** - Offensive or harmful material
- **Personal information** - Sharing others' private information
- **Spam** - Repetitive or unwanted content

## Compliance Requirements

- **Permits required** - Some items need proper documentation
- **State regulations** - Must comply with local laws
- **Compliance review** - Some listings require admin approval
- **Documentation** - Provide required permits/certificates

## Consequences

- **Listing removal** - Prohibited listings are removed
- **Account restrictions** - Repeated violations may restrict account
- **Legal action** - Serious violations may be reported to authorities

## Before Listing

- **Check regulations** for your item type
- **Obtain permits** if required
- **Verify legality** in your location
- **Read listing guidelines** carefully

## Reporting Violations

- **Report listings** that violate rules
- **Contact support** if you see prohibited items
- **Help keep platform safe** for everyone

## Questions?

- **Contact support** if unsure about an item
- **Check local laws** before listing
- **When in doubt**, don't list it
