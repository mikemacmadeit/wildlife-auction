# Buyer Safety Tips

How to stay safe when buying on Agchange.

## Before You Buy

### Research the Seller

**Check seller information:**
- **Verification status** - Look for verified badges
- **Seller ratings** - Read reviews if available
- **Account age** - How long they've been selling
- **Completed sales** - Number of successful sales
- **Response rate** - How quickly they respond

**Red flags:**
- New account with no history
- Unverified seller
- Poor communication
- Suspicious behavior
- Pressure to buy quickly

### Research the Item

**Before buying:**
- **Read listing carefully** - Understand what you're buying
- **Ask questions** - Contact seller with concerns
- **Check photos** - Look at all photos carefully
- **Verify details** - Confirm important information
- **Research market value** - Know what it's worth

**What to ask:**
- Health status and records (animals)
- Condition and specifications (equipment)
- Delivery options and costs
- Any issues or concerns
- Documentation included

## Payment Safety

### Always Use Platform Payment

**NEVER:**
- ❌ Pay outside the platform
- ❌ Send money via wire transfer directly to seller
- ❌ Use cash, Venmo, PayPal (outside platform)
- ❌ Send gift cards or cryptocurrency
- ❌ Pay before seeing item (except through platform)

**ALWAYS:**
- ✅ Use Agchange checkout
- ✅ Pay through platform payment system
- ✅ Use protected payment methods
- ✅ Keep payment on platform
- ✅ Wait for delivery before confirming

### Payment Protection

**How you're protected:**
- Funds held until delivery confirmed
- Inspection period to verify item
- Dispute process if issues arise
- Refund available if problems
- Secure payment processing

**Why it matters:**
- Platform can help if issues arise
- Payment is tracked and protected
- Can get refund if needed
- Seller can't disappear with money
- Professional dispute resolution

## Communication Safety

### Use Platform Messaging

**Stay on platform:**
- Use Agchange messaging
- Keep communication on-platform
- Don't share personal contact info unnecessarily
- Report suspicious messages
- Save important conversations

**Why:**
- Platform can see messages if needed
- Evidence for disputes
- Safer than off-platform communication
- Platform can help if issues arise
- Professional communication

### Red Flags in Communication

**Watch for:**
- Seller asks for payment outside platform
- Pressure to buy quickly
- Refusal to answer questions
- Suspicious requests
- Unprofessional behavior
- Requests for personal information

**What to do:**
- Don't proceed with purchase
- Report to support
- Block seller if needed
- Look for other listings

## Delivery Safety

### Safe Delivery Practices

**For pickup:**
- **Meet in public** - Safe, public location
- **Bring someone** - Don't go alone
- **Inspect before paying** - Verify item first
- **Verify seller identity** - Make sure it's the right person
- **Trust your instincts** - If something seems off, don't proceed

**For delivery:**
- **Verify delivery address** - Make sure it's correct
- **Be available** - Be there for delivery
- **Inspect immediately** - Check item when received
- **Document issues** - Take photos if problems
- **Don't confirm** until satisfied

### Delivery Red Flags

**Watch for:**
- Seller refuses to use platform delivery
- Requests payment before delivery
- Unusual delivery arrangements
- Pressure to confirm before inspection
- Refusal to provide tracking

## Inspection and Verification

### Inspect Thoroughly

**When you receive item:**
- **Inspect immediately** - Don't wait
- **Check against listing** - Compare to description
- **Take photos** - Document condition
- **Test if possible** - Make sure it works
- **Verify paperwork** - Check documentation

**What to check:**
- Matches listing description
- Condition as described
- All features present
- No undisclosed damage
- Documentation included (if mentioned)

### If Item Doesn't Match

**What to do:**
1. **Don't confirm delivery** - Keep funds held
2. **Contact seller immediately** - Explain the issue
3. **Take photos** - Document the problem
4. **Request resolution** - Refund or replacement
5. **Open dispute** - If seller won't help

## Dispute and Resolution

### When to Open Dispute

**Open dispute if:**
- Item not as described
- Item not received
- Item damaged
- Seller unresponsive
- Seller refuses to resolve

**How to open:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on the order
3. Click "Open Dispute"
4. Select reason
5. Provide details and evidence
6. Submit dispute

### Dispute Best Practices

**To win dispute:**
- **Provide clear evidence** - Photos, messages, screenshots
- **Be specific** - Explain exactly what's wrong
- **Be honest** - Don't exaggerate
- **Respond promptly** - Answer admin questions quickly
- **Be professional** - Polite and respectful

## Reporting Issues

### When to Report

**Report to support if:**
- Seller is fraudulent
- Listing is misleading
- Seller is harassing you
- Payment issues
- Safety concerns

**How to report:**
- Click Help button
- Select "Contact Support"
- Fill out support ticket
- Include details and evidence
- Submit report

## General Safety Tips

### Account Security

**Protect your account:**
- Use strong, unique password
- Don't share your password
- Enable two-factor authentication if available
- Log out on shared computers
- Monitor account activity

### Trust Your Instincts

**If something seems off:**
- Don't proceed with purchase
- Ask more questions
- Research seller more
- Contact support
- Look for other listings

### Stay Informed

**Keep learning:**
- Read platform policies
- Check seller reviews
- Ask questions
- Use Help Center
- Contact support when needed
