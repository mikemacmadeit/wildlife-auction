# Trust and Verification

Agchange uses verification and trust features to keep the platform safe.

## Seller Verification

**Verified Sellers:**
- Completed identity verification
- Provided business information (if applicable)
- Met platform requirements
- Display verification badge

**Benefits:**
- Builds buyer confidence
- Higher visibility in search
- Access to more features

## Buyer Protection

- **Secure payments** - Processed by Stripe; Agchange does not hold funds or condition payouts on delivery
- **Dispute resolution** - Platform helps resolve issues
- **Protection window** - Time to inspect purchases
- **Refund process** - For eligible cases

## Trust Indicators

**Look for:**
- Verification badges
- Seller ratings and reviews
- Response time
- Transaction history
- Account age

## Building Trust

**For Sellers:**
- Complete verification
- Provide accurate listings
- Respond promptly to messages
- Maintain good ratings

**For Buyers:**
- Verify your account
- Complete profile
- Communicate clearly
- Leave honest reviews

## Safety Tips

- **Verify seller** before large purchases
- **Read reviews** and ratings
- **Ask questions** before buying
- **Use platform messaging** (not external)
- **Report suspicious activity**

## Reporting Issues

- **Report scams** immediately
- **Contact support** for help
- **Provide details** when reporting
- **Help keep platform safe**
