# Reporting Violations and Issues

How to report problems, violations, or suspicious activity.

## What to Report

### Prohibited Items
- **Illegal items** - Items that violate laws
- **Endangered species** - Without proper permits
- **Stolen property** - Known or suspected stolen items
- **Counterfeit items** - Fake or replica items

### Fraud or Scams
- **Suspicious sellers** - Unusual behavior or requests
- **Payment scams** - Requests for off-platform payment
- **Misrepresentation** - False or misleading listings
- **Phishing attempts** - Suspicious messages or links

### Harassment or Abuse
- **Inappropriate messages** - Harassing or abusive communication
- **Threats** - Any threatening behavior
- **Discrimination** - Discriminatory language or behavior

### Other Issues
- **Technical problems** - Site bugs or errors
- **Account issues** - Problems with your account
- **Policy violations** - Any rule violations

## How to Report

### Report a Listing
1. **Go to the listing page**
2. **Click "Report" button** (if available)
3. **Select reason** for reporting
4. **Provide details** about the issue
5. **Submit report**

### Report a User
1. **Go to user's profile** or message thread
2. **Click "Report"** or contact support
3. **Explain the issue** clearly
4. **Provide evidence** if available
5. **Submit report**

### Contact Support
1. **Use Help widget** - Bottom-right of screen
2. **Click "Contact Support"**
3. **Fill out ticket form**
4. **Describe the issue** in detail
5. **Submit ticket**

## What to Include

- **Clear description** - Explain what's wrong
- **Evidence** - Screenshots, photos, messages
- **Relevant IDs** - Listing ID, user ID, order number
- **Context** - When and how the issue occurred

## After Reporting

- **Report reviewed** - Admin team reviews your report
- **Action taken** - If violation confirmed, action is taken
- **You may be contacted** - For additional information
- **Confidential** - Your report is kept confidential

## Important Notes

- **Report promptly** - Don't wait to report issues
- **Be accurate** - False reports can have consequences
- **Provide evidence** - Helps resolve issues faster
- **Follow up** - Check status if needed

## Safety Tips

- **Trust your instincts** - If something seems wrong, report it
- **Don't engage** - Don't argue with violators, just report
- **Keep evidence** - Save screenshots and messages
- **Help keep platform safe** - Reporting helps everyone
