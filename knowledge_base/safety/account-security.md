# Account Security Best Practices

Keep your account secure with these best practices.

## Password Security

### Strong Passwords
- **Use unique password** - Don't reuse passwords
- **Mix characters** - Letters, numbers, symbols
- **Long enough** - At least 8 characters, longer is better
- **Avoid personal info** - Don't use name, birthday, etc.

### Password Management
- **Don't share** - Never share your password
- **Change regularly** - Update password periodically
- **Use password manager** - Store passwords securely
- **Don't write down** - Avoid physical notes

## Account Protection

### Email Verification
- **Verify email** - Required for account security
- **Keep email secure** - Protect email account
- **Update if needed** - Change email if compromised
- **Monitor email** - Watch for suspicious activity

### Two-Factor Authentication
- **Enable if available** - Extra layer of security
- **Use authenticator app** - More secure than SMS
- **Keep backup codes** - Store in safe place
- **Update regularly** - Keep 2FA current

## Safe Practices

### Login Security
- **Don't share account** - Keep account private
- **Log out** - Especially on shared devices
- **Check activity** - Review account activity regularly
- **Report suspicious** - Contact support if issues

### Transaction Security
- **Use official checkout** - Never pay outside platform
- **Verify sellers** - Check seller ratings
- **Read listings** - Understand what you're buying
- **Keep records** - Save transaction confirmations

## Warning Signs

### Account Compromise
- **Unexpected activity** - Orders you didn't make
- **Password changed** - Without your action
- **Email changed** - Without your knowledge
- **Suspicious messages** - From your account

### What to Do
- **Change password** - Immediately
- **Contact support** - Report the issue
- **Check account** - Review all activity
- **Secure email** - Ensure email is secure

## Phishing Protection

### Recognize Phishing
- **Suspicious emails** - Asking for password
- **Fake links** - URLs that look wrong
- **Urgent requests** - Pressure to act quickly
- **Unexpected requests** - Asking for personal info

### How to Protect
- **Verify sender** - Check email address
- **Don't click links** - Go to site directly
- **Report phishing** - Forward to support
- **When in doubt** - Contact support directly

## Important Notes

- **Platform never asks** for password via email
- **Always use official site** - Don't trust links in emails
- **Keep software updated** - Browser, OS, etc.
- **Be cautious** - If something seems wrong, it probably is
