# Payment Methods

Wildlife Exchange accepts multiple payment methods for your convenience.

## Available Payment Methods

### Credit/Debit Card
- **Fastest option** - Instant processing
- **Accepted cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Secure processing** through Stripe
- **Available to all buyers**

### ACH Debit
- **Bank transfer** from your checking account
- **Lower fees** than card payments
- **Requires verified email**
- **Processing time**: 3-5 business days

### Wire Transfer
- **Direct bank transfer**
- **For larger purchases**
- **Requires verified email**
- **Processing time**: 1-3 business days

## Choosing a Payment Method

**Use Card if:**
- You want instant processing
- Purchase is under $10,000
- You prefer convenience

**Use ACH if:**
- You want lower fees
- You don't mind waiting a few days
- Purchase is moderate amount

**Use Wire if:**
- Purchase is very large
- You prefer bank-to-bank transfer
- You have wire transfer capability

## Payment Security

- **All payments are secure** and encrypted
- **Funds are held** until delivery is confirmed
- **Buyer protection** applies to all payment methods
- **No payment info stored** on our servers

## Payment Processing

- **Card**: Processed immediately
- **ACH**: 3-5 business days
- **Wire**: 1-3 business days

## Troubleshooting

**Payment declined?**
- Check card details are correct
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact your bank if needed
- Try a different payment method

**Payment pending?**
- ACH and wire transfers take time
- Check your bank account
- Contact support if delayed beyond expected time
