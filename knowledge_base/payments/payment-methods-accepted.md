# What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

Agchange accepts multiple secure payment methods for your convenience.

## Accepted Payment Methods

### 1. Credit and Debit Cards

**Supported cards:**
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

**How it works:**
- Enter card details during checkout
- Payment is processed immediately
- Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed
- Works for all listing types (auction, fixed price, best offer)

**Requirements:**
- Valid card with sufficient funds
- Card must not be expired
- Billing address must match card

**Processing time:**
- Instant processing
- Order confirmed immediately after payment

### 2. ACH (Bank Transfer)

**What is ACH:**
- Direct bank-to-bank transfer
- Uses your checking or savings account
- Secure and reliable

**How it works:**
1. Select "ACH" as payment method during checkout
2. Enter your bank account details (routing number, account number)
3. Verify your account (may require micro-deposits)
4. Complete the transfer
5. Payment processes in 3-5 business days

**Requirements:**
- Verified email address
- Valid checking or savings account
- Account must be in your name
- May require account verification

**Processing time:**
- 3-5 business days
- Order is created but payment pending until ACH clears
- Seller is notified when payment clears

**Benefits:**
- Lower fees than credit cards
- Direct from your bank account
- Secure and protected

### 3. Wire Transfer

**What is wire transfer:**
- Direct bank-to-bank transfer
- Faster than ACH
- Typically used for large purchases

**How it works:**
1. Select "Wire Transfer" during checkout
2. Receive wire transfer instructions
3. Complete transfer at your bank
4. Provide transfer confirmation
5. Payment processes in 1-3 business days

**Requirements:**
- Verified email address
- Bank account that supports wire transfers
- May have minimum amount requirements
- Bank may charge wire transfer fees

**Processing time:**
- 1-3 business days
- Faster than ACH
- Order created when transfer is initiated

**When to use:**
- Large purchases (may have lower fees)
- When you need faster processing than ACH
- Preferred by some sellers for high-value items

## Payment Security

**All payment methods are:**
- Encrypted and secure
- Processed through trusted payment processors
- Protected by industry-standard security
- Never stored on Agchange servers

**Your payment information:**
- Is encrypted during transmission
- Is handled by secure payment processors (Stripe)
- Is never shared with sellers
- Is protected by fraud detection

## Choosing a Payment Method

**Use Credit/Debit Card if:**
- You want instant processing
- You prefer convenience
- You want buyer protection from your card issuer
- You're making a smaller purchase

**Use ACH if:**
- You want lower fees
- You don't mind waiting 3-5 days
- You're comfortable with bank transfers
- You're making a medium-sized purchase

**Use Wire Transfer if:**
- You're making a large purchase
- You need faster processing than ACH
- Your bank supports wire transfers
- Seller prefers wire transfer

## Payment Processing

**How payments are handled:**
1. **Payment is processed** when you complete checkout
2. **Funds are held securely** by Agchange
3. **Seller is notified** of your purchase
4. **Delivery is arranged** between you and seller
5. **Funds are released** to seller when you confirm delivery
6. **You have protection** during the delivery window

**Payment protection:**
- Payments are processed through the platform and released according to marketplace confirmation and dispute rules
- Dispute process available if issues arise
- Refund available if item not as described
- Secure delayed settlement protection

## Troubleshooting

**Payment declined?**
- Check card/bank account details are correct
- Verify sufficient funds available
- Check card hasn't expired
- Contact your bank if issues persist
- Try a different payment method

**ACH not working?**
- Make sure email is verified
- Check bank account details are correct
- Verify account is in your name
- Complete account verification if required
- Contact support if problems continue

**Wire transfer questions?**
- Contact support for wire transfer instructions
- Verify bank account details with your bank
- Check for any minimum amount requirements
- Allow 1-3 business days for processing

## Fees

**Credit/Debit Cards:**
- Standard processing fees apply
- Fees are included in the total shown
- No additional fees from Agchange

**ACH:**
- Lower fees than credit cards
- Fees are included in the total
- May have minimum amount requirements

**Wire Transfer:**
- Fees vary by bank
- Your bank may charge wire transfer fees
- Check with your bank for exact fees
