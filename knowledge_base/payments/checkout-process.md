# Checkout Process

Understanding the checkout process for purchases.

## Starting Checkout

### Fixed Price Listings
- **Click "Buy Now"** on listing page
- **Review order details** - Item, price, seller
- **Proceed to checkout**

### Auction Wins
- **Receive win notification** - Email and in-app
- **Go to "My Orders"** - Find your won auction
- **Click "Complete Purchase"** - Start checkout

### Best Offers
- **Offer accepted** - You'll be notified
- **Complete checkout** - Within specified time
- **Proceed to payment**

## Checkout Steps

1. **Review Order**
   - Verify item details
   - Check price and fees
   - Confirm seller information

2. **Select Payment Method**
   - Credit/Debit card
   - ACH debit
   - Wire transfer

3. **Enter Payment Details**
   - Card information (if using card)
   - Bank account (if using ACH/wire)
   - Billing address

4. **Review and Confirm**
   - Double-check all information
   - Review total amount
   - Confirm purchase

5. **Complete Payment**
   - Payment is processed
   - Order is confirmed
   - You'll receive confirmation email

## Important Notes

- **Time limits** - Complete checkout within deadline (for auctions)
- **Payment security** - All payments are encrypted and secure
- **Payments** - Processed by Stripe; we do not hold funds or condition payouts on delivery
- **Order confirmation** - Save confirmation for your records

## After Checkout

- **Order created** - Appears in "My Orders"
- **Payment processed** - Via Stripe; we do not hold funds
- **Seller notified** - Seller knows about purchase
- **Delivery arranged** - Seller will contact you

## Troubleshooting

**Can't complete checkout?**
- Check payment method details
- Verify account has sufficient funds
- Try different payment method
- Contact support if issues persist

**Payment declined?**
- Check card/bank account details
- Verify sufficient funds
- Contact your bank
- Try different payment method
