# Understanding Payouts (Sellers)

How sellers receive payment for their sales.

## When Payouts Are Released

- **After delivery confirmation** - Buyer confirms receipt
- **After protection window** - For protected transactions
- **After dispute resolution** - If dispute is resolved in your favor
- **Admin approval** - Some transactions require admin approval

## Payout Process

1. **Sale completes** - Buyer makes purchase
2. **Delivery confirmed** - Buyer confirms receipt
3. **Protection window** - If applicable, wait for window to end
4. **Payout released** - Funds transferred to your account
5. **Processing time** - 1-3 business days typically

## Payout Amount

- **Sale price** - Full amount buyer paid
- **Minus platform fee** - 4-7% based on your tier
- **Minus payment processing** - Small processing fee
- **Net amount** - What you actually receive

## Payment Account

- **Must be set up** - Required to receive payouts
- **Verified account** - Account must be verified
- **Current information** - Keep account details updated
- **Secure transfer** - Funds transferred directly to your account

## Payout Timing

- **Standard transactions** - Usually 1-3 business days after release
- **Protected transactions** - After protection window ends
- **Disputed transactions** - After dispute resolution
- **Weekends/holidays** - May delay processing

## Tracking Payouts

- **Seller Dashboard** - See payout status
- **Transaction history** - View all payouts
- **Email notifications** - Receive payout confirmations
- **Account statements** - Download for records

## Important Notes

- **Payouts are final** - Cannot be reversed after release
- **Tax responsibility** - You're responsible for reporting income
- **Keep records** - Save payout information for taxes
- **Contact support** - If payout is delayed or missing
