# How Refunds Work

Complete guide to understanding refunds on Wildlife Exchange.

## When You Can Get a Refund

### Valid Reasons for Refunds

**Item not as described:**
- Significantly different from listing
- Missing important features
- Wrong item received
- Condition misrepresented

**Item not received:**
- Order never arrived
- Delivery failed
- Item lost in transit
- Seller can't deliver

**Item damaged:**
- Damage during shipping
- Damage not disclosed
- Arrived broken or unusable
- Significant defects

**Seller issues:**
- Seller is unresponsive
- Seller refuses to resolve
- Seller can't fulfill order
- Seller behavior concerns

**Order cancelled:**
- Cancelled before delivery
- Both parties agree to cancel
- Admin-approved cancellation

### When Refunds Are NOT Available

**Buyer's remorse:**
- Simply changed your mind
- Found better deal elsewhere
- No real issue with item
- Personal preference change

**Minor issues:**
- Small cosmetic differences
- Minor variations from photos
- Things you should have asked about
- Normal wear and tear

**Outside protection window:**
- Too much time has passed
- Protection period expired
- Item already confirmed delivered
- Order completed long ago

## How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Contact the Seller

**Always try seller first:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on the order
3. Click "Message Seller"
4. Explain the issue clearly
5. Request refund or resolution
6. Give seller 24-48 hours to respond

**What to include:**
- Clear description of the issue
- Photos if relevant (damage, wrong item, etc.)
- What you'd like (refund, replacement, etc.)
- Be polite and professional

### Step 2: If Seller Doesn't Help

**If seller won't resolve:**
- Wait 48 hours for response
- Send follow-up message
- If still no help, proceed to dispute

### Step 3: Open a Dispute

**How to open dispute:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Click on the order
3. Click "Open Dispute"
4. Select dispute reason:
   - Item not as described
   - Item not received
   - Item damaged
   - Other issue
5. Provide details and evidence
6. Submit dispute

**What to include:**
- Clear explanation of the issue
- Photos or screenshots as evidence
- Messages with seller (if relevant)
- Any other relevant information

## Refund Process

### After Dispute Is Opened

**What happens:**
1. **Admin reviews** your dispute
2. **Seller is notified** and can respond
3. **Evidence is reviewed** from both sides
4. **Decision is made** - Usually within a few business days
5. **You're notified** of the decision

### If Refund Is Approved

**Refund processing:**
- **Full refund** - If item not received or significantly different
- **Partial refund** - If item has issues but still usable
- **Refund method** - Same as original payment method
- **Processing time** - 5-10 business days typically

**Refund timeline:**
- **Credit/Debit Card:** 5-7 business days
- **ACH:** 7-10 business days
- **Wire Transfer:** 7-10 business days
- **Check your account** after processing time

### If Refund Is Denied

**Why refunds might be denied:**
- Issue doesn't meet refund criteria
- Evidence doesn't support claim
- Outside protection window
- Buyer's remorse situation

**What you can do:**
- Review the decision explanation
- Provide additional evidence if available
- Contact support for clarification
- Accept the decision if fair

## Refund Amounts

### Full Refund

**When you get full refund:**
- Item not received
- Item significantly not as described
- Item unusable or broken
- Seller can't fulfill order

**Amount:**
- Full purchase price
- May include shipping costs
- Processing fees may not be refunded
- Check dispute decision for exact amount

### Partial Refund

**When you get partial refund:**
- Item has issues but still usable
- Minor differences from listing
- Some features missing
- Negotiated resolution

**Amount:**
- Percentage of purchase price
- Based on issue severity
- Agreed upon in dispute resolution
- Check dispute decision for exact amount

## Refund Methods

### Credit/Debit Card

**How it works:**
- Refunded to same card
- Appears as credit on statement
- 5-7 business days typically
- Check your card statement

### ACH (Bank Transfer)

**How it works:**
- Refunded to same bank account
- 7-10 business days typically
- Check your bank account
- May take longer for some banks

### Wire Transfer

**How it works:**
- Refunded via wire transfer
- 7-10 business days typically
- May require bank account details
- Check with your bank

## Timeline

**Typical refund timeline:**
1. **Contact seller** - 1-2 days
2. **Open dispute** (if needed) - Same day
3. **Admin review** - 2-5 business days
4. **Decision made** - Notification sent
5. **Refund processed** - 5-10 business days
6. **Total:** 1-3 weeks typically

**Faster if:**
- Seller agrees immediately
- Clear evidence provided
- Simple case
- Both parties cooperate

**Slower if:**
- Complex case
- Need more evidence
- Seller disputes claim
- Requires investigation

## Tips for Successful Refunds

**To get a refund:**
- **Contact seller first** - Most issues resolved this way
- **Be clear and specific** - Explain the issue clearly
- **Provide evidence** - Photos, messages, screenshots
- **Be reasonable** - Ask for appropriate resolution
- **Be patient** - Process takes time

**Document everything:**
- Save messages with seller
- Take photos of issues
- Keep order confirmation
- Note dates and times
- Save any relevant information

## Prevention

**To avoid needing refunds:**
- **Ask questions** before buying
- **Read listing carefully** - Understand what you're buying
- **Check seller reputation** - Review ratings and verification
- **Inspect item** when received
- **Confirm delivery** only when satisfied
