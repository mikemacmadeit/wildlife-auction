# Payment Security and Fraud Prevention

How Wildlife Exchange keeps your payments secure.

## Security Measures

### Encryption
- **All transactions encrypted** - Data protected in transit
- **Secure connections** - HTTPS for all payment pages
- **Industry standards** - Meets PCI compliance requirements
- **No storage** - Payment details not stored on our servers

### Payment Processing
- **Trusted providers** - Uses established payment processors
- **Secure handling** - Payment data handled securely
- **Fraud detection** - Automated systems detect suspicious activity
- **Verification** - Additional checks for large transactions

## For Buyers

### Protecting Yourself
- **Use official checkout** - Never pay sellers directly
- **Verify seller** - Check seller ratings and verification
- **Check listing** - Review item details carefully
- **Report suspicious** - Contact support if something seems wrong

### Red Flags
- **Off-platform payment** - Seller asks for payment outside platform
- **Too good to be true** - Suspiciously low prices
- **Pressure tactics** - Seller rushing you to pay
- **Unusual requests** - Asking for unusual payment methods

## For Sellers

### Protecting Yourself
- **Use platform** - Process all payments through platform
- **Verify buyers** - Check buyer accounts and history
- **Wait for confirmation** - Don't ship until payment confirmed
- **Report issues** - Contact support for suspicious activity

### Red Flags
- **Payment outside platform** - Buyer wants to pay directly
- **Overpayment scams** - Buyer sends too much and asks for refund
- **Fake payment confirmations** - Verify payments are real
- **Suspicious accounts** - New accounts with no history

## What to Do

### If You Suspect Fraud
- **Don't proceed** - Stop the transaction
- **Report immediately** - Contact support right away
- **Provide details** - Include all relevant information
- **Save evidence** - Screenshots, messages, etc.

### If You're a Victim
- **Contact support** - Report the fraud immediately
- **Provide evidence** - All relevant information
- **Cooperate** - Work with support to resolve
- **Change passwords** - If account compromised

## Platform Protection

- **Fraud monitoring** - Automated systems watch for fraud
- **Account verification** - Helps prevent fake accounts
- **Transaction limits** - Limits on new accounts
- **Support team** - Available to help with issues

## Important Notes

- **Never share** payment details outside platform
- **Use official channels** - Only use platform checkout
- **Verify everything** - Check seller/buyer before transacting
- **Report suspicious** - Help keep platform safe
