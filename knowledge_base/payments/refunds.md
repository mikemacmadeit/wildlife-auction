# Refunds and Returns

Understanding when and how refunds work on Wildlife Exchange.

## When Refunds Are Available

- **Item not as described** - Significant differences from listing
- **Item not received** - Non-delivery or delivery issues
- **Damaged during shipping** - Item arrived damaged
- **Seller cancellation** - Seller cancels the order
- **Dispute resolution** - Admin determines refund is appropriate

## Refund Process

1. **Contact seller first** - Try to resolve the issue
2. **Open a dispute** if seller doesn't respond or resolve
3. **Provide evidence** - Photos, messages, documentation
4. **Admin reviews** - Platform reviews the case
5. **Decision made** - Refund approved or denied
6. **Refund processed** - Funds returned to original payment method

## Refund Timing

- **Processing time**: 5-10 business days after approval
- **Method dependent**: Card refunds faster than ACH/wire
- **Original method**: Refund goes to same payment method used

## Partial Refunds

- **Available in some cases** - If item is partially acceptable
- **Admin determines** - Based on evidence and circumstances
- **Seller agreement** - May be required in some cases

## What's Not Refundable

- **Buyer's remorse** - Changed your mind
- **Outside protection window** - Too much time has passed
- **Minor differences** - Small variations from listing
- **Prohibited transactions** - Violations of terms

## Return Shipping

- **Buyer responsibility** in most cases
- **Seller may offer** to cover return shipping
- **Discuss with seller** before returning

## Tips

- **Inspect items promptly** upon delivery
- **Document issues** with photos
- **Contact seller first** before opening dispute
- **Keep all communication** on the platform
