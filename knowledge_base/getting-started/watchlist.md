# Using the Watchlist

Save listings you're interested in to your watchlist for easy access later.

## Adding to Watchlist

- **Click "Add to Watchlist"** on any listing
- **Quick access** to items you're considering
- **No limit** on number of items
- **Private** - Only you can see your watchlist

## Viewing Your Watchlist

- **Go to Dashboard** â†’ **Watchlist**
- **See all saved items** in one place
- **Filter and sort** your watchlist
- **Remove items** when no longer interested

## Watchlist Features

- **Notifications** - Get alerts for price changes, ending soon
- **Quick actions** - Place bid, make offer, contact seller
- **Organization** - Keep track of items you're watching
- **Comparison** - Compare multiple items easily

## Managing Your Watchlist

- **Remove items** - Click remove when done
- **Clear all** - Remove all items at once
- **Export** - Download list as CSV (if available)

## Tips

- **Add items** you're seriously considering
- **Review regularly** - Remove items you're no longer interested in
- **Use notifications** - Stay updated on watched items
- **Compare items** - Use watchlist to compare options
