# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Answers to the most common questions about Agchange.

## General Questions

### What is Agchange?

Agchange is a trusted online marketplace for buying and selling animals, livestock, ranch equipment, and related items. We connect buyers and sellers in a secure, transparent platform with buyer and seller protection.

### Is Agchange free to use?

**For buyers:** Yes, creating an account and browsing is free. You only pay when you make a purchase.

**For sellers:** Creating an account and listing items is free. Fees are only charged when your item sells (typically 5-10% of sale price).

### Do I need to verify my email?

Yes, email verification is required to:
- Place bids and make purchases
- Create listings (sellers)
- Use ACH and wire transfer payments
- Access all platform features

### How do I verify my email?

1. Check your email inbox after signing up
2. Look for verification email from Agchange
3. Check spam folder if not in inbox
4. Click the verification link
5. You'll be redirected and verified

## Buying Questions

### How do I buy something?

1. Browse or search for items
2. Click on a listing to view details
3. Contact seller with questions (optional but recommended)
4. Click "Buy Now" (fixed price) or place bid (auction)
5. Complete checkout with payment
6. Arrange delivery with seller
7. Confirm delivery when you receive item

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) - Instant
- ACH Bank Transfer - 3-5 business days
- Wire Transfer - 1-3 business days

### How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary:
- Local pickup: Same day to 3 days
- Local delivery: 1-5 days
- Animal transport: 3-14 days
- Equipment shipping: 5-14 business days

### What if I don't receive my order?

1. Contact seller first (wait 24-48 hours for response)
2. Check order status in "My Orders"
3. Verify delivery address is correct
4. Contact support if seller doesn't help
5. Open a dispute if needed

### Can I cancel an order?

**Before payment clears:** Yes, you can cancel.

**After payment:** You need seller's agreement or valid reason (item not as described, not received, etc.).

**How to cancel:**
- Go to "My Orders"
- Click on the order
- Request cancellation from seller or contact support

### How do I get a refund?

1. Contact seller first to resolve
2. If seller won't help, open a dispute
3. Admin reviews dispute
4. Refund processed if approved
5. Takes 5-10 business days typically

## Selling Questions

### How do I list an item for sale?

1. Sign in and go to "Create Listing"
2. Choose listing type (Auction or Fixed Price)
3. Select category
4. Add title, description, photos
5. Set location and pricing
6. Fill in attributes
7. Review and publish
8. Admin reviews (usually within 24 hours)

### What are the seller fees?

- Platform fee: 5-10% of final sale price (depends on seller tier)
- Payment processing: ~2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe fees)
- Fees only charged when item sells
- No listing fees

### When do I get paid?

- After buyer confirms delivery
- Funds released to your payment account
- Processing: 1-3 business days
- Total: Usually 5-10 business days from sale

### Why isn't my listing showing?

**Common reasons:**
- Still under admin review (wait 24-48 hours)
- Listing was rejected (check for admin notes)
- Listing status is "Draft" (need to publish)
- Search filters are hiding it
- Email not verified

### How do I contact a buyer?

- Go to "My Orders" or "My Listings"
- Click on the order or listing
- Click "Message Buyer" or "Contact Buyer"
- Send your message
- Buyer will be notified

## Account Questions

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to sign-in page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password

### How do I update my profile?

1. Go to Account Settings
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update information
4. Click "Save Changes"

### How do I change my email?

1. Go to Account Settings
2. Find email section
3. Enter new email
4. Verify new email address
5. Update is complete

## Safety Questions

### Is Agchange safe?

Yes, we prioritize safety:
- Verified accounts
- Secure payments
- Buyer and seller protection
- Dispute resolution
- Reporting system

### How do I stay safe when buying?

- Use platform messaging
- Never pay outside the platform
- Verify seller information
- Inspect item before confirming delivery
- Report suspicious behavior

### What if I see a suspicious listing?

- Click "Report" on the listing (if available)
- Contact support with details
- Include listing ID and reason
- We'll investigate promptly

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge
- Most modern browsers

### Can I use Agchange on mobile?

Yes, Agchange works on:
- Mobile browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
- Responsive design for mobile

### Why is something not working?

- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection
- Make sure browser is updated
- Disable browser extensions
- Contact support if issues persist
