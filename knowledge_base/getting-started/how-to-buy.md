# How to Buy on Wildlife Exchange

Complete guide to buying animals, livestock, equipment, and more on Wildlife Exchange.

## Getting Started

**Before you buy:**
- Create an account (free and quick)
- Verify your email address (required for purchases)
- Complete your profile (helps sellers trust you)
- Have payment method ready (card, bank account, or wire transfer)

## Finding Items to Buy

### Browse Listings

1. **Go to Browse page** - Click "Browse" in navigation
2. **Use search** - Enter keywords (e.g., "quarter horse", "cattle", "trailer")
3. **Apply filters** - Filter by:
   - Category (Wildlife, Cattle, Horses, Equipment, etc.)
   - Location (state, region)
   - Price range
   - Listing type (Auction, Fixed Price, Best Offer)
   - Other category-specific filters

4. **View listings** - Click on any listing to see details
5. **Save favorites** - Click heart icon to save listings to watchlist

### Search Tips

- **Be specific** - More specific searches get better results
- **Try different terms** - "horse" vs "equine" vs "equestrian"
- **Use filters** - Narrow down by location, price, type
- **Check regularly** - New listings added daily

## Understanding Listing Types

### Fixed Price Listings

**How it works:**
- Price is set by seller
- You pay the listed price
- Purchase is immediate
- No bidding required

**Steps to buy:**
1. Click **"Buy Now"** button on listing
2. Review order details (item, price, seller)
3. Select payment method
4. Enter payment information
5. Complete checkout
6. Order is confirmed

**Best for:**
- When you know you want it
- When price is acceptable
- When you want immediate purchase
- No time to wait for auction

### Auction Listings

**How it works:**
- Buyers place bids
- Highest bid wins when auction ends
- You can set a maximum bid (proxy bidding)
- Auction has a set end time

**Steps to buy:**
1. **Place a bid:**
   - Enter your bid amount
   - Or set maximum bid (you'll bid automatically up to this)
   - Confirm your bid
   
2. **Monitor the auction:**
   - Watch for other bids
   - You'll be notified if outbid
   - You can increase your bid anytime
   
3. **If you win:**
   - You'll receive win notification (email + in-app)
   - Go to "My Orders" to complete checkout
   - Complete checkout within deadline (usually 48 hours)
   - Payment is processed

**Best for:**
- When you want to get the best price
- When you're flexible on timing
- When you enjoy the bidding process
- When item is in high demand

### Best Offer Listings

**How it works:**
- Make an offer below asking price
- Seller can accept, counter, or decline
- Negotiate until agreement or decline
- If accepted, complete checkout

**Steps to buy:**
1. Click **"Make Offer"** button
2. Enter your offer amount
3. Add optional message to seller
4. Submit your offer
5. Wait for seller response (usually 24-48 hours)
6. If accepted, complete checkout
7. If countered, accept counter or make new offer

**Best for:**
- When you want to negotiate price
- When listing price is flexible
- When you want to discuss terms
- When seller is open to offers

## Making Your Purchase

### Step 1: Review the Listing

**Before buying, check:**
- ✅ Item description matches what you want
- ✅ Photos show the item clearly
- ✅ Price is acceptable
- ✅ Seller information (verification, location, ratings)
- ✅ Delivery/pickup options
- ✅ Any special requirements or disclosures

### Step 2: Contact Seller (Optional but Recommended)

**Good questions to ask:**
- Health status and veterinary records (for animals)
- Age, breed, training level (for animals)
- Condition and specifications (for equipment)
- Delivery options and costs
- Availability for pickup
- Any questions about the item

**How to contact:**
- Click "Contact Seller" on listing page
- Send a message with your questions
- Wait for seller response (usually 24-48 hours)

### Step 3: Complete Checkout

**For Fixed Price:**
1. Click "Buy Now"
2. Review order summary
3. Select payment method
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm purchase

**For Auction Win:**
1. Go to "My Orders"
2. Find your won auction
3. Click "Complete Purchase"
4. Select payment method
5. Enter payment details
6. Confirm purchase

**For Accepted Offer:**
1. Go to "My Orders" or notification
2. Click "Complete Purchase"
3. Select payment method
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm purchase

### Step 4: Payment

**Payment methods:**
- **Credit/Debit Card** - Instant processing
- **ACH (Bank Transfer)** - 3-5 business days
- **Wire Transfer** - 1-3 business days

**Payment security:**
- All payments are encrypted and secure
- Funds held until delivery confirmed
- Protected transaction process
- Refund available if issues arise

## After Your Purchase

### Immediate (Day 1)

- **Order confirmation** - You'll receive email confirmation
- **Order appears** in "My Orders" dashboard
- **Seller is notified** of your purchase
- **Payment is processed** (or pending for ACH/wire)

### Within 24-48 Hours

- **Seller should contact you** about delivery
- **Discuss delivery options** - Pickup, seller delivery, or transport
- **Arrange delivery/pickup** - Set date, time, location
- **Stay in communication** - Respond to seller messages

### Delivery

- **Receive the item** - Pickup, delivery, or transport
- **Inspect the item** - Make sure it's as described
- **Confirm delivery** - Click "Confirm Delivery" in order
- **Funds released** - Seller receives payment after confirmation

## Your Protection

**Buyer protection includes:**
- **Funds held securely** - Not released until delivery confirmed
- **Inspection period** - Time to verify item is as described
- **Dispute process** - If item not as described or issues arise
- **Refund available** - If problems can't be resolved
- **Secure payments** - All transactions are protected

## Tips for Successful Purchases

**Before buying:**
- Ask questions - Contact seller with any concerns
- Review seller profile - Check verification, ratings, location
- Read listing carefully - Understand what you're buying
- Check delivery options - Make sure you can receive it

**During purchase:**
- Complete checkout promptly - Especially for auctions
- Respond to seller messages - Good communication is key
- Be flexible with delivery - Work with seller on timing

**After purchase:**
- Stay in communication - Keep seller updated
- Inspect item carefully - Before confirming delivery
- Confirm delivery promptly - So seller can get paid
- Leave feedback - Help other buyers

## Troubleshooting

**Can't complete checkout?**
- Make sure email is verified
- Check payment method details are correct
- Verify sufficient funds available
- Try different payment method
- Contact support if issues persist

**Seller not responding?**
- Wait 24-48 hours (sellers may be busy)
- Send a follow-up message
- Check order status
- Contact support if no response after 3 days

**Item not as described?**
- Contact seller first to resolve
- Document the issue (photos, messages)
- Open a dispute if seller won't help
- Contact support for assistance
