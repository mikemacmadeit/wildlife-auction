# How to Buy on Wildlife Exchange

Buying on Wildlife Exchange is straightforward. Here's how the process works.

## Finding Items

1. **Browse listings** - Use the search and filter tools to find what you're looking for
2. **View listing details** - Click on any listing to see full information, photos, and seller details
3. **Check seller information** - Review seller ratings, verification status, and location

## Making a Purchase

### Fixed Price Listings

1. Click **"Buy Now"** on the listing page
2. Review the order details
3. Select payment method (card, ACH, or wire transfer)
4. Complete checkout
5. Payment is processed securely

### Auction Listings

1. Place a bid by entering your bid amount
2. If you win, you'll be notified when the auction ends
3. Complete checkout within the specified time
4. Payment is processed securely

### Best Offer Listings

1. Click **"Make Offer"** on the listing
2. Enter your offer amount
3. Seller can accept, counter, or decline
4. If accepted, complete checkout

## After Purchase

- You'll receive an order confirmation email
- Track your order status in "My Orders"
- Communicate with the seller through the order page
- Confirm delivery when you receive the item

## Important Notes

- Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed
- You have a protection window to inspect your purchase
- Contact support if you have any issues
