# What is Wildlife Exchange?

Wildlife Exchange is a marketplace for buying and selling wildlife, livestock, ranch equipment, and related items. We connect buyers and sellers in a secure, transparent platform.

## Key Features

- **Secure Transactions**: Protected payment system ensures funds are held safely until delivery is confirmed
- **Multiple Listing Types**: Auctions, fixed-price listings, and classified ads
- **Compliance Support**: Built-in compliance checks for regulated items
- **Trust & Safety**: Verified sellers, dispute resolution, and buyer protection

## Who Can Use Wildlife Exchange?

- **Buyers**: Anyone looking to purchase wildlife, livestock, or ranch equipment
- **Sellers**: Breeders, ranchers, and businesses selling related items

## Getting Started

1. Create an account (free)
2. Complete your profile
3. Browse listings or create your first listing
4. Start buying or selling
