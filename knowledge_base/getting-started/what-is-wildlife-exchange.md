# What Is Wildlife Exchange?

Wildlife Exchange is a trusted online marketplace for buying and selling animals, livestock, ranch equipment, and related items. We connect buyers and sellers in a secure, transparent platform.

## What We Are

**Wildlife Exchange is:**
- An online marketplace for animals and livestock
- A platform for ranch equipment and related items
- A secure place to buy and sell with protection
- A community of buyers and sellers
- A trusted platform with verification and safety measures

**We're NOT:**
- A pet store or retail shop
- A broker or middleman (we're the platform)
- A replacement for veterinary care
- A legal advisor (we don't provide legal advice)

## What You Can Buy

**Animals & Livestock:**
- Wildlife & Exotics - Game animals, exotic species
- Cattle & Livestock - Cattle, goats, sheep, pigs, etc.
- Horses & Equestrian - Horses, ponies, donkeys, mules
- Sporting & Working Dogs - Hunting dogs, working breeds

**Equipment & Supplies:**
- Ranch Equipment - Tractors, trailers, tools, fencing
- Ranch Vehicles - Trucks, ATVs, utility vehicles
- Hunting & Outfitter Assets - Hunting equipment, gear

## How It Works

### For Buyers

1. **Browse listings** - Search and filter to find what you want
2. **Contact sellers** - Ask questions before buying
3. **Make purchase** - Buy now, bid in auction, or make an offer
4. **Secure payment** - Payment held until delivery confirmed
5. **Receive item** - Pickup, delivery, or professional transport
6. **Confirm delivery** - Inspect and confirm receipt
7. **Funds released** - Seller gets paid after confirmation

### For Sellers

1. **Create account** - Sign up and verify your email
2. **Complete profile** - Add business info, location, verification
3. **Set up payments** - Connect Stripe to receive payouts
4. **Create listing** - Add photos, description, pricing
5. **Publish listing** - Admin reviews, then goes live
6. **Sell your item** - Receive bids, offers, or direct purchases
7. **Arrange delivery** - Coordinate with buyer
8. **Get paid** - Receive payout after delivery confirmed

## Key Features

### Security & Protection

**Buyer Protection:**
- Funds held securely until delivery confirmed
- Inspection period to verify item
- Dispute process if issues arise
- Refund available if problems can't be resolved

**Seller Protection:**
- Verified buyers
- Secure payment processing
- Dispute resolution process
- Payment held until delivery confirmed

### Verification & Trust

**Seller Verification:**
- Email verification required
- Profile completion
- Payment account verification
- Trust badges for verified sellers

**Buyer Verification:**
- Email verification required
- Account verification
- Payment method verification

### Multiple Listing Types

**Auction:**
- Buyers bid, highest bid wins
- Set starting bid and reserve price
- Auction runs for set duration
- Automatic bidding (proxy bidding)

**Fixed Price:**
- Set a price, buyer pays immediately
- Optional "Best Offer" feature
- Instant purchase available
- No waiting for auction to end

**Best Offer:**
- Negotiate price with seller
- Seller can accept, counter, or decline
- Flexible pricing options
- Can combine with fixed price

### Payment Options

**Accepted Methods:**
- Credit and Debit Cards (instant)
- ACH Bank Transfer (3-5 days)
- Wire Transfer (1-3 days)

**All payments are:**
- Encrypted and secure
- Processed through trusted processors
- Protected by fraud detection
- Held until delivery confirmed
