# How to Contact a Seller

Learn how to reach out to sellers with questions about their listings.

## Ways to Contact Sellers

### Method 1: Through the Listing Page

**Step-by-step:**
1. **Go to the listing page** - Click on any listing you're interested in
2. **Look for "Contact Seller" or "Message Seller" button** - Usually near the listing details
3. **Click the button** - This opens a message form or chat
4. **Write your message** - Ask your questions clearly
5. **Send the message** - Seller will be notified

**What to include in your message:**
- Your question or what you'd like to know
- Any specific details you need
- Your contact preference if needed
- Be polite and professional

### Method 2: Through Your Order (After Purchase)

**If you've already purchased:**
1. **Go to "My Orders"** in your dashboard
2. **Click on the order** you want to discuss
3. **Look for "Message Seller" or "Contact Seller"** button
4. **Send your message** - Seller will receive notification

**When to use this:**
- Questions about delivery
- Order status updates
- Delivery arrangements
- Issues with your order

### Method 3: Through Messages (If Conversation Started)

**If you've already messaged the seller:**
1. **Go to "Messages"** in your dashboard
2. **Find the conversation** with the seller
3. **Click to open** the conversation
4. **Send your message**

## What to Ask Sellers

**Good questions to ask:**
- **About the animal/item:**
  - Health status and veterinary records
  - Age, breed, or specific characteristics
  - Training level or experience
  - Any special needs or requirements
  - Documentation or paperwork included

- **About delivery/pickup:**
  - Where is the item located?
  - Can you deliver? What are delivery options?
  - When can pickup be arranged?
  - What are delivery costs?

- **About the sale:**
  - Is the price negotiable?
  - Are you open to offers?
  - What payment methods do you accept?
  - When do you need payment?

- **About the seller:**
  - How long have you been selling?
  - Can you provide references?
  - What's your return policy?

## Communication Tips

**Be professional:**
- Use polite, respectful language
- Be clear about what you're asking
- Respond promptly to seller messages
- Be honest about your intentions

**Be specific:**
- Ask specific questions, not vague ones
- Mention the listing you're asking about
- Include relevant details in your message

**Be patient:**
- Sellers may take 24-48 hours to respond
- They may be busy or away from their computer
- Follow up politely if no response after 2-3 days

## What Sellers Can See

**Sellers can see:**
- Your message/question
- Your profile information (if you're signed in)
- Your purchase history (if relevant)
- Your verification status

**Sellers cannot see:**
- Your exact location (unless you share it)
- Your payment information
- Your personal contact info (unless you share it)

## Troubleshooting

**Can't find "Contact Seller" button?**
- Make sure you're signed in to your account
- Check that the listing is still active
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if button is missing

**Seller not responding?**
- Wait 24-48 hours (sellers may be busy)
- Send a polite follow-up message
- Check if listing is still active
- Consider contacting support if urgent

**Want to share contact info?**
- You can share phone number or email in messages
- Be cautious about sharing personal information
- Use Agchange messaging when possible (safer)

## Safety Tips

**Stay safe when contacting sellers:**
- **Use Agchange messaging** - Keeps communication on-platform
- **Don't share payment info** - Never send money outside the platform
- **Meet in public** - If meeting in person, choose a safe location
- **Trust your instincts** - If something seems off, be cautious
- **Report suspicious behavior** - Contact support if seller seems fraudulent

**Red flags to watch for:**
- Seller asks for payment outside the platform
- Seller refuses to answer questions
- Seller pressures you to buy quickly
- Seller's information doesn't match listing
- Seller asks for personal information unnecessarily
