# How to Sign Up for an Account

Creating an account on Wildlife Exchange is free and takes just a few minutes.

## Steps to Sign Up

1. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top navigation
2. **Enter your email address** - Use a valid email you can access
3. **Create a password** - Choose a strong, unique password
4. **Verify your email** - Check your inbox for a verification link
5. **Complete your profile** - Add your name, location, and other details

## What You'll Need

- A valid email address
- A secure password
- Basic information (name, location)

## After Signing Up

- Browse listings immediately
- Add items to your watchlist
- Contact sellers
- Create listings (after profile completion)

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive verification email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Can't sign in?**
- Make sure you verified your email
- Check that you're using the correct email and password
- Use the "Forgot Password" link if needed
