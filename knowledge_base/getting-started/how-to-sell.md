# How to Sell on Wildlife Exchange

Selling on Wildlife Exchange allows you to reach buyers across the platform. Here's how to get started.

## Before You List

1. **Complete your profile** - Add business information, location, and verification details
2. **Set up payment account** - Connect your payment account to receive payouts
3. **Understand fees** - Review platform fees based on your seller tier

## Creating a Listing

1. Click **"Sell"** in the navigation
2. Choose listing type:
   - **Auction**: Bidders compete, highest bid wins
   - **Fixed Price**: Set a price, buyer pays immediately
3. Fill in listing details:
   - Title and description
   - Photos (at least one required)
   - Price or starting bid
   - Location
   - Category-specific attributes
4. Review and publish

## Listing Requirements

- Clear, accurate title and description
- High-quality photos
- Accurate pricing
- Correct category and attributes
- Valid location information

## After Publishing

- Your listing appears in search results
- Buyers can contact you with questions
- You'll receive notifications for bids, offers, and purchases
- Manage your listings from the Seller Dashboard

## Getting Paid

- Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed
- Payouts are released after buyer acceptance
- Processing time varies by payment method
