# How to Sell on Agchange

Complete guide to becoming a successful seller on Agchange.

## Getting Started as a Seller

**Before you can sell, you need to:**
1. ✅ **Create an account** - Sign up for free
2. ✅ **Verify your email** - Required to publish listings
3. ✅ **Complete your profile** - Add name, location, phone
4. ✅ **Set up payment account** - Connect Stripe to receive payouts
5. ✅ **Understand the process** - Learn how selling works

**Why these are required:**
- Email verification ensures account security
- Complete profile builds trust with buyers
- Payment account is how you receive money
- All help you sell successfully

## Step 1: Complete Your Seller Profile

**Go to Account Settings:**
1. Click your profile/avatar
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Fill in required information

**Required information:**
- **Full Name** - Your real name (buyers see this)
- **Location** - City and state (helps local buyers find you)
- **Phone Number** - For delivery coordination (highly recommended)

**Additional (recommended):**
- **Business Name** - If selling as a business
- **Profile Description** - Tell buyers about yourself
- **Profile Photo** - Builds trust and credibility

**Why it matters:**
- Buyers trust sellers with complete profiles
- Verified sellers get more interest
- Complete profiles rank better in search
- Builds your seller reputation

## Step 2: Set Up Payment Account

**Required to receive payouts:**
- Must connect Stripe payment account
- Required before you can publish listings
- Secure and industry-standard

**How to set up:**
1. Go to Seller Dashboard or Account Settings
2. Find "Payment Account" or "Stripe" section
3. Click "Connect Stripe" or "Set Up Payments"
4. Complete Stripe onboarding:
   - Business or personal information
   - Bank account details
   - Tax information (EIN or SSN)
   - Identity verification
5. Complete and verify setup

**What you'll need:**
- Business name (or personal name if individual)
- Bank account (checking or savings)
- Tax ID (EIN for business, SSN for individual)
- Business address
- Identity verification documents

**Stripe is secure:**
- Used by millions of businesses worldwide
- Industry-standard security
- Your bank details are encrypted
- Agchange never sees your bank information

## Step 3: Understand Listing Types

### Auction Listings

**How it works:**
- Set a starting bid price
- Buyers compete by bidding
- Highest bid wins when auction ends
- Optional reserve price (minimum you'll accept)

**Best for:**
- Items with uncertain market value
- When you want competitive bidding
- High-demand items
- When you're flexible on final price

**Tips:**
- Set competitive starting bid
- Consider setting a reserve price
- Choose appropriate auction duration
- Monitor bidding activity

### Fixed Price Listings

**How it works:**
- Set a specific price
- Buyers pay that price immediately
- No bidding or waiting
- Optional "Best Offer" feature

**Best for:**
- When you know the price you want
- When you want immediate sale
- Items with clear market value
- When you want to avoid auction uncertainty

**Tips:**
- Research similar listings for pricing
- Price competitively but fairly
- Consider enabling "Best Offer"
- Be ready to respond to offers

### Best Offer Feature

**How it works:**
- Buyers can make offers below asking price
- You can accept, counter, or decline
- Negotiate until agreement
- If accepted, buyer completes checkout

**Best for:**
- When price is somewhat flexible
- When you want to negotiate
- Items that may need price discussion
- Building relationships with buyers

## Step 4: Create Your First Listing

### Choose Category

**Select the right category:**
- **Registered & Specialty Livestock** - Game animals, registered ranch species
- **Cattle & Livestock** - Cattle, goats, sheep, pigs
- **Horses & Equestrian** - Horses, ponies, donkeys
- **Ranch Equipment** - Tractors, trailers, tools
- **Ranch Vehicles** - Trucks, ATVs, utility vehicles
- **Sporting & Working Dogs** - Hunting dogs, working breeds
- **Hunting & Outfitter Assets** - Hunting equipment

**Why category matters:**
- Helps buyers find your listing
- Ensures proper attributes are available
- Affects search visibility
- Important for compliance

### Write Your Listing

**Title:**
- Be specific and descriptive
- Include key details (age, breed, condition)
- Example: "6 Year Old Grade Mare - Broke to Ride"
- Keep under 100 characters
- Make it searchable

**Description:**
- Write detailed, honest description
- Include health information (for animals)
- Mention training, temperament, experience
- List any paperwork or documentation
- Be transparent about any issues
- Minimum 50 characters, recommended 200-500 words

**Photos:**
- Upload at least 1 photo (required)
- 3-5 photos recommended
- High quality, clear, well-lit
- Show different angles
- Include close-ups of important details
- First photo is the main listing image

**Pricing:**
- **Auction:** Set starting bid and optional reserve
- **Fixed Price:** Set your asking price
- Research similar listings for competitive pricing
- Consider market value and condition

**Location:**
- Enter your city and state
- Helps buyers find local listings
- Required for delivery coordination
- Be accurate for pickup arrangements

**Attributes:**
- Fill in category-specific attributes
- Species, breed, age, health (for animals)
- Specifications, condition (for equipment)
- Any other relevant details

### Review and Publish

**Before publishing, check:**
- ✅ All information is accurate
- ✅ Photos are clear and show item well
- ✅ Price is set correctly
- ✅ Location is correct
- ✅ Description is complete and honest
- ✅ Required fields are filled

**To publish:**
1. Click "Publish Listing" button
2. Listing goes to admin review
3. Admin reviews (usually within 24 hours)
4. You'll be notified when approved or if changes needed
5. Once approved, listing goes live

## Step 5: Manage Your Listings

**After publishing:**
- View in "My Listings" dashboard
- Track views and interest
- Respond to buyer questions
- Edit listing if needed (before it sells)
- Monitor bids and offers

**When you get a sale:**
- You'll be notified (email + in-app)
- Contact buyer within 24-48 hours
- Arrange delivery/pickup
- Coordinate with buyer
- Confirm delivery when complete
- Receive payout after confirmation

## Getting Paid

### Payment Process

**How it works:**
1. Buyer makes purchase
2. Payment is processed by Stripe (sellers receive funds when buyer pays; we do not hold or release payouts)
3. You arrange delivery with buyer
4. Buyer receives and inspects item
5. Buyer confirms delivery (if applicable)
6. Dispute window applies; Agchange does not condition payouts on delivery

**Timeline:**
- Sellers receive funds via Stripe when the buyer pays
- Processing: 1-3 business days typically for bank transfers
- Agchange does not hold funds or delay payouts

### Understanding Fees

**Platform fees:**
- Charged only when item sells
- Percentage of final sale price
- Typically 5-10% depending on your tier
- Deducted automatically from sale

**Payment processing:**
- Stripe charges processing fees
- Typically 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- Standard for online payments
- Deducted from your payout

**What you receive:**
- Sale price
- Minus platform fee
- Minus payment processing fee
- Equals your payout

## Common Questions

**How long does admin review take?**
- Usually 24-48 hours
- May be faster or slower depending on volume
- You'll be notified when reviewed

**Can I edit my listing after publishing?**
- Yes, before it sells
- Go to "My Listings" and click "Edit"
- Changes may require re-review

**What if my listing doesn't sell?**
- No fees if it doesn't sell
- You can relist or edit and republish
- Try adjusting price or description

**How do I know if someone is interested?**
- You'll get notifications for:
  - Messages from buyers
  - Bids (auctions)
  - Offers (best offer)
  - Watchers/favorites

## Troubleshooting

**Can't publish listing?**
- Make sure email is verified
- Check payment account is connected
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Contact support if issues persist

**Listing not showing after approval?**
- Wait 5-10 minutes for indexing
- Check listing status is "Active"
- Try searching for your listing
- Contact support if not appearing

**Need help?**
- Check Help Center articles
- Use AI chat for questions
- Contact support for assistance
- Seller dashboard has resources
