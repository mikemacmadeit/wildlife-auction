# What Happens When You Win an Auction

Congratulations! Here's what to expect after winning an auction.

## Immediate Steps

1. **You'll receive a notification** (email and in-app)
2. **Go to "My Orders"** to see your purchase
3. **Complete checkout** within the specified time
4. **Payment is processed** securely

## Checkout Process

1. **Review order details** - Verify item, price, and seller
2. **Select payment method** - Card, ACH, or wire transfer
3. **Complete payment** - Funds are held securely
4. **Order confirmed** - You'll receive confirmation

## Time Limits

- **Checkout deadline** - Usually 24-48 hours after auction ends
- **Complete payment** before deadline
- **Contact support** if you need more time

## After Payment

- **Funds are held securely** until delivery
- **Seller arranges delivery** or pickup
- **You can message seller** through the order page
- **Track order status** in "My Orders"

## If You Don't Complete Checkout

- **Order may be cancelled** if deadline passes
- **Seller may relist** the item
- **Contact support** immediately if you need help

## Delivery

- **Seller will contact you** to arrange delivery
- **Confirm delivery** when you receive the item
- **Protection window** starts after delivery confirmation
