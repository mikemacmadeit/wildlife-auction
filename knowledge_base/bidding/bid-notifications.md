# Bid Notifications

Stay informed about bidding activity with notifications.

## For Buyers

### When You're Notified

- **You're outbid** - Someone placed a higher bid
- **Auction ending soon** - Reminder before auction closes
- **You won** - Notification when auction ends and you're the winner
- **Reserve met** - When your bid meets the reserve price

### Notification Methods

- **Email** - Sent to your registered email
- **In-app** - Appears in notification center
- **Both** - Can receive both types

### Managing Notifications

- **Settings** - Control which notifications you receive
- **Frequency** - Choose real-time or digest
- **Disable** - Turn off specific notification types

## For Sellers

### When You're Notified

- **New bid placed** - Every time someone bids
- **Reserve met** - When reserve price is reached
- **Auction ending soon** - Reminder before close
- **Auction ended** - Notification when auction closes

### Benefits

- **Stay engaged** - Know when there's activity
- **Respond to questions** - Buyers may message during bidding
- **Monitor progress** - Track how your auction is doing
