# Placing a Bid

Here's how to place a bid on an auction listing.

## Before Bidding

- **Verify your email** (required)
- **Review the listing** carefully
- **Check seller information** and ratings
- **Know your maximum budget**

## Steps to Place a Bid

1. **Go to the auction listing**
2. **Enter your bid amount** in the bid box
3. **Review the bid amount** (make sure it's correct)
4. **Click "Place Bid"**
5. **Confirm your bid**

## Bid Amount Rules

- Must be **higher than current bid**
- Must meet **minimum increment** (usually $1 or $5)
- Cannot bid **less than starting bid** (if no bids yet)

## After Placing a Bid

- You'll see confirmation if you're the high bidder
- You'll be notified if someone outbids you
- Check back before auction ends to maintain your position

## Bid History

- View all bids on the listing page
- See who placed bids (usernames only)
- Track bid amounts and timing

## Important Notes

- **Bids are binding** - you're committed if you win
- **Cannot retract bids** once placed
- **Winning bid** must complete purchase
- **Reserve price** may prevent sale even if you win

## Troubleshooting

**Can't place bid?**
- Verify your email address
- Check your account status
- Ensure bid amount is high enough
- Contact support if issues persist
