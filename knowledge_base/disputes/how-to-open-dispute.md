# How to Open a Dispute

Step-by-step guide to opening a dispute if you have issues with your purchase.

## Before You Start

- **Contact seller first** - Always try to resolve directly
- **Gather evidence** - Photos, messages, documentation
- **Know your issue** - Be clear about what's wrong
- **Check protection window** - Make sure you're within the time limit

## Steps to Open a Dispute

1. **Go to "My Orders"** in your dashboard
2. **Find the order** with the issue
3. **Click "Open Dispute"** button
4. **Select dispute reason:**
   - Item not as described
   - Item not received
   - Damaged item
   - Other (explain)
5. **Provide details:**
   - What's wrong
   - When you noticed the issue
   - What you've tried to resolve it
6. **Upload evidence:**
   - Photos showing the issue
   - Screenshots of messages
   - Any relevant documentation
7. **Submit dispute**

## Required Information

- **Dispute reason** - Why you're opening the dispute
- **Description** - Detailed explanation of the issue
- **Evidence** - Photos or documentation
- **Contact attempts** - What you've tried with seller

## After Submitting

- **Dispute is reviewed** by admin team
- **Seller is notified** and can respond
- **You'll receive updates** via email and in-app
- **Decision is made** based on evidence

## Providing Evidence

**Good evidence includes:**
- Clear photos showing the problem
- Comparison photos (listing vs. received)
- Screenshots of messages with seller
- Documentation (receipts, tracking, etc.)

**Tips:**
- Take photos in good lighting
- Show multiple angles if needed
- Include timestamps if relevant
- Be honest and accurate

## Timeline

- **Review starts**: Within 1-2 business days
- **Seller response**: Usually 2-3 business days
- **Decision**: Typically within 5-7 business days
- **Resolution**: Depends on decision type
