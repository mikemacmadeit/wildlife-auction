# Dispute Resolution Process

How disputes are reviewed and resolved on Wildlife Exchange.

## Process Overview

1. **Dispute opened** - Buyer or seller opens dispute
2. **Evidence submitted** - Both parties provide evidence
3. **Admin review** - Platform reviews the case
4. **Decision made** - Resolution determined
5. **Implementation** - Decision is carried out

## For Buyers

### Opening a Dispute

- **Go to your order** - Find the order with issue
- **Click "Open Dispute"** - Start the process
- **Provide details** - Explain the problem
- **Submit evidence** - Photos, messages, documentation

### During Review

- **Respond promptly** - If admin asks questions
- **Provide additional info** - If requested
- **Be honest** - Accurate information helps
- **Be patient** - Reviews take time

### Possible Outcomes

- **Full refund** - Complete refund of purchase price
- **Partial refund** - Partial refund if item partially acceptable
- **No refund** - If dispute not valid
- **Other resolution** - Case-by-case basis

## For Sellers

### Responding to Disputes

- **You'll be notified** - Email and in-app notification
- **Review the dispute** - Understand buyer's complaint
- **Provide your side** - Explain your perspective
- **Submit evidence** - Photos, messages, documentation

### During Review

- **Respond promptly** - Don't delay response
- **Be professional** - Keep communication respectful
- **Provide evidence** - Support your position
- **Cooperate** - Work with admin to resolve

### Possible Outcomes

- **Dispute dismissed** - If buyer's claim not valid
- **Partial refund** - If item partially acceptable
- **Full refund** - If buyer's claim is valid
- **Other resolution** - Case-by-case basis

## Timeline

- **Initial review** - 1-2 business days
- **Evidence gathering** - 2-3 business days
- **Decision** - Usually within 5-7 business days
- **Resolution** - Depends on decision type

## Important Notes

- **Be honest** - False claims can result in account restrictions
- **Provide evidence** - Strong evidence helps your case
- **Respond promptly** - Delays can affect outcome
- **Accept decisions** - Admin decisions are final
