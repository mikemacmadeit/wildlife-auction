# Responding to Disputes (Sellers)

How to respond effectively if a buyer opens a dispute.

## When Dispute Is Opened

- **You'll be notified** - Email and in-app notification
- **Review the claim** - Understand what buyer is claiming
- **Don't panic** - Most disputes can be resolved
- **Respond promptly** - Time limits may apply

## How to Respond

### Step 1: Review the Dispute
- **Read carefully** - Understand buyer's complaint
- **Check order details** - Review what was sold
- **Gather your evidence** - Photos, messages, documentation
- **Understand the issue** - What exactly is the problem?

### Step 2: Provide Your Side
- **Be professional** - Keep communication respectful
- **Be factual** - Stick to facts, not emotions
- **Be clear** - Explain your perspective clearly
- **Be honest** - Don't make false claims

### Step 3: Submit Evidence
- **Photos** - Show item condition when shipped
- **Messages** - Screenshots of communication
- **Documentation** - Receipts, tracking, certificates
- **Any relevant info** - Whatever supports your case

## What to Include

### Good Evidence
- **Photos of item** - As it was when shipped
- **Packaging photos** - Show how it was packed
- **Communication** - Messages showing buyer was informed
- **Tracking info** - Proof of delivery
- **Listing details** - What was actually listed

### Professional Response
- **Acknowledge concern** - Show you take it seriously
- **Explain your position** - Your side of the story
- **Provide evidence** - Support your position
- **Propose solution** - If appropriate

## Common Dispute Types

### Item Not as Described
- **Show listing** - What was actually described
- **Photos** - Item matches description
- **Clarify** - Explain any misunderstandings

### Item Not Received
- **Tracking proof** - Show delivery confirmation
- **Delivery address** - Verify correct address used
- **Contact attempts** - Show you tried to resolve

### Damaged Item
- **Packaging photos** - Show it was packed well
- **Shipping method** - How it was shipped
- **Insurance** - If item was insured

## Best Practices

- **Respond quickly** - Don't delay your response
- **Be professional** - Keep it respectful
- **Provide evidence** - Strong evidence helps
- **Stay calm** - Don't get defensive
- **Work with admin** - Cooperate with review process

## Possible Outcomes

- **Dispute dismissed** - If buyer's claim not valid
- **Partial resolution** - Compromise solution
- **Full refund** - If buyer's claim is valid
- **Other resolution** - Case-by-case basis

## Prevention

- **Accurate listings** - Describe items honestly
- **Good photos** - Show item clearly
- **Clear communication** - Answer buyer questions
- **Careful packaging** - Pack items securely
- **Fast shipping** - Ship promptly
