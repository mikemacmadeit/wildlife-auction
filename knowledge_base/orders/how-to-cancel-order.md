# How to Cancel an Order

Learn when and how to cancel an order on Agchange.

## When You Can Cancel

### Buyer Can Cancel If:

**Before payment clears:**
- Payment is still pending (ACH/wire transfers)
- You haven't confirmed payment
- Order is still in "Payment Pending" status

**With seller agreement:**
- Seller agrees to cancel
- Both parties want to cancel
- Arranged through messages

**Special circumstances:**
- Item significantly different from listing
- Seller is unresponsive
- Delivery issues that can't be resolved
- Admin-approved cancellation

### When You Cannot Cancel:

**After payment cleared:**
- Payment has been processed
- Order is confirmed
- Seller has been notified

**After delivery arranged:**
- Delivery/pickup is scheduled
- Seller has made arrangements
- Transport is booked

**After delivery:**
- Item has been delivered
- You've confirmed delivery
- Order is completed

## How to Cancel

### Method 1: Cancel Before Payment Clears

**If payment is still pending (ACH/wire):**
1. Go to "My Orders" in your dashboard
2. Find the order you want to cancel
3. Look for "Cancel Order" button
4. Click to cancel
5. Order will be cancelled and payment won't process

**What happens:**
- Order is cancelled immediately
- Payment won't be processed
- Seller is notified
- No fees charged

### Method 2: Request Cancellation from Seller

**If payment has cleared:**
1. Go to your order page
2. Click "Message Seller"
3. Politely request cancellation
4. Explain your reason
5. Wait for seller's response

**What to say:**
- "Hi, I'd like to cancel this order. [Reason]. Can we arrange a cancellation and refund?"
- Be polite and explain your situation
- Seller may agree to cancel

**If seller agrees:**
- Seller can process refund
- Funds returned to your payment method
- Order is cancelled
- Processing takes 5-10 business days

### Method 3: Contact Support

**If you need help cancelling:**
1. Click Help button (bottom-right)
2. Select "Contact Support"
3. Fill out support ticket
4. Include:
   - Order number
   - Reason for cancellation
   - What you've tried
   - Screenshots if relevant

**Support can help if:**
- Seller is unresponsive
- You have a valid reason to cancel
- There are issues with the order
- You need assistance with the process

## Cancellation Reasons

### Valid Reasons:

**Item issues:**
- Item significantly different from listing
- Item not as described
- Missing important features
- Wrong item received

**Seller issues:**
- Seller is unresponsive
- Seller can't deliver
- Seller refuses to resolve issues
- Seller behavior concerns

**Personal circumstances:**
- Change in circumstances
- Financial situation changed
- Can no longer complete purchase
- Emergency situation

### Not Valid Reasons:

**Buyer's remorse:**
- Simply changed your mind
- Found a better deal elsewhere
- Decided you don't want it
- No real issue with order

**Minor issues:**
- Small differences from photos
- Minor cosmetic issues
- Personal preferences
- Things you should have asked about

## Refund Process

### If Cancellation Is Approved:

**Refund timeline:**
- 5-10 business days typically
- Processed to original payment method
- Credit card: 5-7 business days
- ACH/Wire: 7-10 business days

**What you'll receive:**
- Full refund if cancelled before delivery
- May be partial refund if item was delivered
- Processing fees may not be refunded
- Check with support for specific cases

### Refund Methods:

**Credit/Debit Card:**
- Refunded to same card
- Appears as credit on statement
- 5-7 business days

**ACH:**
- Refunded to same bank account
- 7-10 business days
- Check your bank account

**Wire Transfer:**
- Refunded via wire transfer
- 7-10 business days
- May require bank account details

## After Cancellation

**What happens:**
- Order status changes to "Cancelled"
- Seller is notified
- Refund is processed (if applicable)
- You receive confirmation email

**If you want to buy again:**
- You can create a new order
- Contact seller if interested
- No restrictions on future purchases

## Seller's Perspective

**Sellers can also cancel:**
- If buyer is unresponsive
- If payment issues arise
- If item is no longer available
- With buyer's agreement

**If seller cancels:**
- You'll receive full refund
- Order is cancelled
- You can look for other listings
- No penalty to you

## Disputes vs. Cancellations

**Cancellation:**
- Both parties agree
- Amicable resolution
- Refund processed
- Order ends

**Dispute:**
- One party has an issue
- Admin reviews and decides
- May result in refund or resolution
- More formal process

## Tips

**Before cancelling:**
- Try to resolve issues with seller first
- Be clear about your reason
- Be polite and professional
- Consider if issue can be resolved

**To avoid needing to cancel:**
- Ask questions before buying
- Review listing carefully
- Check seller's reputation
- Verify item details
