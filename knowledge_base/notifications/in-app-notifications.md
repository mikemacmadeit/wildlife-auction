# In-App Notifications

Stay updated on activity through in-app notifications.

## Where to Find Notifications

- **Notification bell** in the top navigation
- **Dashboard** shows recent activity
- **Order pages** show order-specific updates
- **Listing pages** show listing activity

## Types of Notifications

### For Buyers
- Order status updates
- Delivery confirmations
- Dispute updates
- Support replies

### For Sellers
- New bids
- Best offers
- Order updates
- Payment notifications
- Support replies

## Managing Notifications

- **Mark as read** - Click notification to mark read
- **View all** - See full notification history
- **Settings** - Adjust what you're notified about

## Notification Preferences

- **Customize** which notifications you receive
- **Choose delivery method** (email, in-app, or both)
- **Set frequency** for different notification types

## Important Notes

- **Check regularly** for important updates
- **Respond promptly** to time-sensitive notifications
- **Clear old notifications** to keep organized
