# Notification Troubleshooting

Having trouble receiving notifications? Here's how to fix it.

## Not Receiving Email Notifications

### Check Spam Folder
- **Look in spam/junk** - Emails may be filtered
- **Mark as not spam** - If found in spam
- **Add to contacts** - Whitelist our email address
- **Check filters** - Email filters may be blocking

### Verify Email Address
- **Check account settings** - Ensure email is correct
- **Verify email** - Make sure email is verified
- **Update if needed** - Change email if incorrect
- **Re-verify** - Verify new email address

### Check Notification Settings
- **Go to settings** - Account → Notifications
- **Verify enabled** - Make sure notifications are on
- **Check frequency** - May be set to digest mode
- **Enable specific types** - Turn on notifications you want

## Not Receiving In-App Notifications

### Check Settings
- **Notification preferences** - Verify in-app notifications enabled
- **Browser permissions** - Allow browser notifications
- **Check browser** - Some browsers block notifications
- **Try different browser** - See if issue is browser-specific

### Browser Issues
- **Clear cache** - May fix notification issues
- **Update browser** - Use latest version
- **Check permissions** - Browser may be blocking
- **Try incognito** - See if extensions interfering

## Too Many Notifications

### Adjust Settings
- **Go to settings** - Account → Notifications
- **Disable less important** - Turn off what you don't need
- **Use digest mode** - Get summaries instead of individual
- **Set frequency** - Daily or weekly instead of real-time

### Notification Types
- **Disable specific types** - Turn off what's not needed
- **Keep important** - Keep critical notifications on
- **Customize** - Set preferences per notification type

## Notifications Not Working

### General Troubleshooting
- **Check account status** - Ensure account is active
- **Verify email** - Email must be verified
- **Check internet** - Ensure connection is working
- **Try logging out/in** - May reset notification system

### Contact Support
- **If still not working** - Contact support
- **Describe issue** - What notifications you're missing
- **Provide details** - Email, account info, etc.
- **Be patient** - Support will investigate

## Best Practices

- **Check spam regularly** - Don't miss important emails
- **Keep email current** - Update if you change emails
- **Review settings** - Periodically check preferences
- **Test notifications** - Verify they're working
