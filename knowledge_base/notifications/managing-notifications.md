# Managing Notification Settings

Customize which notifications you receive and how.

## Accessing Settings

1. **Go to Account Settings**
2. **Find "Notifications" section**
3. **Adjust preferences**
4. **Save changes**

## Notification Types

### Order Notifications
- **New orders** - When you receive an order
- **Order updates** - Status changes
- **Payment received** - When payment is confirmed
- **Delivery updates** - Delivery status changes

### Bidding Notifications
- **Outbid** - When someone outbids you
- **Auction ending** - Reminders before close
- **You won** - When you win an auction
- **New bids** - On your auctions (sellers)

### Message Notifications
- **New messages** - From buyers/sellers
- **Support replies** - Responses to tickets
- **Offer updates** - Best offer responses

### Account Notifications
- **Security alerts** - Account changes
- **Verification** - Status updates
- **Important updates** - Platform announcements

## Delivery Methods

- **Email** - Sent to your email address
- **In-app** - Appears in notification center
- **Both** - Receive both types
- **None** - Disable specific notifications

## Frequency Options

- **Real-time** - Immediate notifications
- **Daily digest** - Summary once per day
- **Weekly summary** - Summary once per week
- **Disabled** - No notifications for that type

## Best Practices

- **Enable important** - Don't miss critical updates
- **Disable less important** - Reduce notification overload
- **Use digest** - For less urgent notifications
- **Review regularly** - Adjust as needed

## Important Notes

- **Some notifications required** - Cannot be disabled (security, orders)
- **Email verification** - Required for some notification types
- **Test notifications** - Verify settings are working
- **Update preferences** - Change anytime in settings
