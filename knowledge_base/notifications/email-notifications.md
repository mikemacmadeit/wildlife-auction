# Email Notifications

Stay informed about important activity on your account through email notifications.

## What You'll Receive

### For Buyers
- Order confirmations
- Delivery updates
- Dispute notifications
- Support ticket replies
- Auction win notifications

### For Sellers
- New bids on your auctions
- Best offers received
- Order notifications
- Payment updates
- Support ticket replies

## Notification Settings

You can manage your email preferences:
1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Find **"Notifications"** section
3. Toggle notifications on/off
4. Save your preferences

## Important Emails

Some emails are always sent (cannot be disabled):
- Order confirmations
- Payment receipts
- Security alerts
- Account changes

## Email Frequency

- **Real-time** for urgent items (orders, payments)
- **Daily digest** option for less urgent items
- **Weekly summary** available for some notifications

## Troubleshooting

**Not receiving emails?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address is correct
- Check notification settings
- Whitelist our email domain
- Contact support if issues persist

**Too many emails?**
- Adjust notification settings
- Enable digest mode
- Disable less important notifications

## Email Security

- **Never share** email login details
- **Report suspicious emails** immediately
- **Verify sender** before clicking links
- **Use official links** from our website
