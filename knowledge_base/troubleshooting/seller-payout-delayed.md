# Payout Delayed (Sellers)

What to do if your payout is taking longer than expected.

## Normal Processing Times

- **Standard payout** - 1-3 business days after release
- **Protected transactions** - After protection window ends
- **Weekends/holidays** - May add delay
- **Bank processing** - Additional 1-2 days possible

## Common Reasons for Delay

### Protection Window
- **Still active** - Protection window hasn't ended yet
- **Wait for expiration** - Payout released after window
- **Check order status** - See when protection ends

### Delivery Not Confirmed
- **Buyer hasn't confirmed** - Waiting for buyer action
- **Contact buyer** - Remind them to confirm
- **Admin may confirm** - In some cases

### Account Issues
- **Payment account not verified** - Must be verified
- **Account details incorrect** - Check account information
- **Bank account issues** - Verify with your bank

### Dispute or Hold
- **Active dispute** - Payout held during dispute
- **Admin review** - Some transactions require review
- **Compliance check** - May delay payout

## What to Check

1. **Order status** - Is delivery confirmed?
2. **Protection window** - Has it ended?
3. **Payment account** - Is it verified and correct?
4. **Disputes** - Any active disputes?
5. **Email** - Check for notifications

## Solutions

### Verify Account
- **Check payment account** - Ensure it's verified
- **Update if needed** - Correct any errors
- **Re-verify** - If account was changed

### Contact Buyer
- **Politely remind** - Ask buyer to confirm delivery
- **Through platform** - Use order messaging
- **Be professional** - Don't pressure buyer

### Contact Support
- **If delayed beyond normal** - Contact support
- **Provide order number** - Help support find your order
- **Explain situation** - What you've checked
- **Be patient** - Support will investigate

## Prevention

- **Set up account early** - Before creating listings
- **Keep account current** - Update if details change
- **Communicate clearly** - With buyers about delivery
- **Follow up** - Gently remind buyers to confirm

## Important Notes

- **Processing times vary** - Some delays are normal
- **Weekends count** - Business days exclude weekends
- **Bank delays** - Your bank may add processing time
- **Contact support** - If significantly delayed
