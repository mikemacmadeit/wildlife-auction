# Payment Issues

Having trouble with payments? Here are common solutions.

## Payment Declined

### Common Causes
- **Insufficient funds** - Check account balance
- **Card expired** - Update card information
- **Incorrect details** - Verify card number, CVV, expiration
- **Bank restrictions** - Contact your bank

### Solutions
- **Verify card details** are correct
- **Check account balance**
- **Contact your bank** if needed
- **Try different payment method**

## Payment Pending

### ACH/Wire Transfers
- **Normal delay** - Takes 3-5 business days (ACH) or 1-3 days (wire)
- **Check bank account** for processing
- **Contact bank** if delayed beyond expected time

### Card Payments
- **Should be instant** - Contact support if pending
- **Check email** for confirmation
- **Verify payment** in order details

## Payment Not Processing

- **Check internet connection**
- **Try different browser**
- **Clear browser cache**
- **Disable browser extensions**
- **Try different device**

## Refund Questions

- **Processing time**: 5-10 business days
- **Original method**: Refunded to same payment method
- **Check with bank** if not received after 10 days
- **Contact support** if delayed

## Contact Support

If payment issues persist:
- **Provide order number**
- **Describe the issue**
- **Include error messages** (if any)
- **Support will help** resolve the issue
