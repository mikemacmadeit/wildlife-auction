# How to Contact Support

Need help? Here's how to reach our support team.

## Ways to Contact

### Help Widget
- **Click Help button** (bottom-right of screen)
- **"Contact Support" tab**
- **Fill out support ticket form**
- **Submit your question**

### Support Page
- **Go to Dashboard** â†’ **Support**
- **Create new ticket**
- **Track existing tickets**

### Email
- **Send email** to support address
- **Include details** about your issue
- **Provide order/listing IDs** if relevant

## What to Include

**When contacting support:**
- **Clear description** of the issue
- **Relevant IDs** (order number, listing ID, etc.)
- **Screenshots** if helpful
- **Steps you've tried** to resolve
- **Your account email**

## Response Time

- **General inquiries**: 1-2 business days
- **Urgent issues**: Faster response
- **Complex issues**: May take longer
- **Check email** for replies

## Before Contacting

- **Check help articles** - Your question may be answered
- **Try troubleshooting** steps
- **Contact seller first** (for order issues)
- **Gather information** about your issue

## Support Can Help With

- **Account issues** - Login, verification, settings
- **Payment problems** - Checkout, refunds, processing
- **Order questions** - Status, delivery, disputes
- **Listing help** - Creating, editing, visibility
- **Technical issues** - Site problems, errors

## Support Cannot Help With

- **Price negotiations** - Work directly with seller
- **Item condition questions** - Ask seller directly
- **Delivery arrangements** - Coordinate with seller
- **Legal advice** - Consult legal professional

## Tips

- **Be specific** about your issue
- **Be patient** - Support handles many requests
- **Be polite** - Courtesy helps everyone
- **Follow up** if needed (reference ticket number)
