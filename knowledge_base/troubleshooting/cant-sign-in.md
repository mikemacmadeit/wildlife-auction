# Can't Sign In

Having trouble signing in? Here are common solutions.

## Common Issues

### Wrong Password
- **Check caps lock** - Passwords are case-sensitive
- **Verify spelling** - Make sure password is correct
- **Use password reset** if you've forgotten it

### Wrong Email
- **Verify email address** - Make sure it's correct
- **Check for typos** - Common mistake
- **Try different email** if you have multiple accounts

### Account Not Verified
- **Check email** for verification link
- **Verify email address** to activate account
- **Resend verification** if needed

## Solutions

### Reset Password
1. Click **"Forgot Password"** on sign-in page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### Verify Email
1. Check your inbox for verification email
2. Click verification link
3. Try signing in again

### Clear Browser Data
- **Clear cookies** and cache
- **Try different browser**
- **Try incognito/private mode**

## Still Having Issues?

- **Contact support** for assistance
- **Provide email address** you used to sign up
- **Check spam folder** for emails
- **Wait a few minutes** and try again

## Prevention

- **Use password manager** to store passwords
- **Verify email** immediately after sign-up
- **Keep email address updated** in account settings
