# Seller Verification Process

Get verified as a seller to build trust and unlock more features.

## Why Get Verified?

- **Build buyer confidence** - Verified badge shows trust
- **Higher visibility** - Verified sellers appear higher in search
- **More features** - Access to advanced seller tools
- **Better support** - Priority support for verified sellers

## Verification Requirements

- **Complete profile** - All required information filled
- **Identity verification** - Provide identification
- **Business information** - If selling as business
- **Payment account** - Connected and verified

## How to Get Verified

1. **Complete your profile** - Fill in all required fields
2. **Submit verification request** - Go to Account Settings
3. **Provide documents** - Upload required identification
4. **Wait for review** - Usually 1-3 business days
5. **Get verified** - Receive verification badge

## Required Documents

- **Government ID** - Driver's license or passport
- **Business license** - If selling as business (if applicable)
- **Proof of address** - Utility bill or similar
- **Additional docs** - May be required for certain categories

## After Verification

- **Verification badge** appears on your profile
- **Higher search ranking** in results
- **Access to features** reserved for verified sellers
- **Buyer confidence** increases

## Maintaining Verification

- **Keep information current** - Update if details change
- **Follow platform rules** - Violations may affect status
- **Respond to requests** - Platform may request updated docs
