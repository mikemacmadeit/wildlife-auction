# Email Verification

Verifying your email address is required to use certain features on Wildlife Exchange.

## Why Verify Your Email?

- Required for placing bids and making purchases
- Enables ACH and wire transfer payment methods
- Receives important order and account notifications
- Improves account security

## How to Verify

1. **Check your inbox** after signing up
2. **Click the verification link** in the email
3. **You're verified!** - You can now use all platform features

## Didn't Receive the Email?

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (emails can take time to arrive)
- Make sure you used the correct email address
- Request a new verification email from your account settings

## Resend Verification Email

1. Go to your Account Settings
2. Find the "Email Verification" section
3. Click "Resend Verification Email"
4. Check your inbox for the new email

## Troubleshooting

**Link expired?** Request a new verification email.

**Wrong email address?** Update your email in account settings, then verify the new address.

**Still having issues?** Contact support for assistance.
