# Complete Account Setup Guide

Step-by-step guide to setting up your Agchange account completely.

## Why Complete Your Account?

**Benefits of complete account:**
- **Verified sellers** get more trust from buyers
- **Complete profiles** help buyers find you
- **Verified email** required for purchases and listings
- **Payment account** required to receive payouts (sellers)
- **Better experience** - Access to all features

## Step 1: Sign Up

**Create your account:**
1. Click "Sign Up" in top navigation
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a secure password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for verification link

**Password tips:**
- Use at least 8 characters
- Mix of letters and numbers
- Avoid common words
- Use a unique password (not used elsewhere)
- Consider a password manager

## Step 2: Verify Your Email

**Why verify:**
- Required to place bids and make purchases
- Required to create listings (sellers)
- Enables ACH and wire transfer payments
- Receives important notifications

**How to verify:**
1. Check your email inbox
2. Look for email from Agchange
3. Click the verification link
4. You'll be redirected and verified

**Didn't receive email?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 2-5 minutes (emails can be delayed)
- Make sure email address is correct
- Request new verification email from account settings

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

**Go to Account Settings:**
1. Click your profile/avatar in top right
2. Select "Account Settings" or "Profile"
3. Fill in your information

**Required information:**
- **Full Name** - Your real name (buyers and sellers see this)
- **Location** - City and state (helps with local transactions)
- **Phone Number** - For delivery coordination (optional but recommended)

**Additional information (sellers):**
- **Business Name** - If selling as a business
- **Business Address** - For business listings
- **Description** - Tell buyers about yourself
- **Profile Photo** - Builds trust with buyers

**Why complete profile:**
- Buyers trust sellers with complete profiles
- Helps with local transactions
- Required for certain features
- Better seller reputation

## Step 4: Set Up Payment Account (Sellers)

**Required for sellers:**
- Must be set up to receive payouts
- Required before you can publish listings
- Secure connection through Stripe

**How to set up:**
1. Go to Seller Dashboard or Account Settings
2. Find "Payment Account" or "Stripe" section
3. Click "Connect Stripe" or "Set Up Payments"
4. Complete Stripe onboarding:
   - Business information
   - Bank account details
   - Identity verification
   - Tax information
5. Complete setup

**What you'll need:**
- Business name (or personal name)
- Bank account information
- Tax ID (EIN or SSN)
- Business address
- Identity verification documents

**Stripe is secure:**
- Industry-standard security
- Used by millions of businesses
- Your information is encrypted
- Agchange never sees your bank details

## Step 5: Understand Account Types

**Buyer Account:**
- Can browse and purchase
- Can place bids
- Can make offers
- Can contact sellers
- Can open disputes

**Seller Account:**
- Everything buyer can do, plus:
- Create listings
- Manage listings
- Receive payouts
- Seller dashboard access
- Seller tools and analytics

**You can be both:**
- Same account works for buying and selling
- Switch between buyer and seller features
- No need for separate accounts

## Step 6: Notification Preferences

**Set up notifications:**
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Find "Notifications" section
3. Choose your preferences:
   - Email notifications
   - In-app notifications
   - Push notifications (mobile)

**What you can get notified about:**
- Order updates
- Bid activity
- Offer responses
- Messages from sellers/buyers
- Listing activity
- Account changes

## Step 7: Security Settings

**Secure your account:**
- **Strong password** - Use a unique, strong password
- **Password manager** - Store passwords securely
- **Two-factor authentication** - If available, enable it
- **Regular updates** - Keep email and phone updated
- **Monitor activity** - Check account regularly

**Security tips:**
- Never share your password
- Use different password than other sites
- Log out on shared computers
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Verification Status

**Check your verification:**
- Go to Account Settings
- Look for verification badges:
  - ✅ Email Verified
  - ✅ Profile Complete
  - ✅ Payment Account Connected (sellers)
  - ✅ Seller Verified (sellers)

**What each means:**
- **Email Verified** - You can buy and sell
- **Profile Complete** - You've added all required info
- **Payment Account** - You can receive payouts (sellers)
- **Seller Verified** - You're a trusted seller

## Troubleshooting

**Can't verify email?**
- Check spam folder
- Request new verification email
- Make sure email is correct
- Contact support if issues persist

**Can't complete profile?**
- Make sure all required fields are filled
- Check for error messages
- Try different browser
- Contact support if problems

**Can't connect payment account?**
- Make sure email is verified first
- Have all required information ready
- Complete Stripe onboarding fully
- Contact support if Stripe setup fails

**Account not working?**
- Make sure email is verified
- Check that profile is complete
- Verify payment account (sellers)
- Contact support for help

## Next Steps

**After account setup:**
- **Buyers:** Start browsing and making purchases
- **Sellers:** Create your first listing
- **Both:** Explore the platform features
- **Questions?** Use Help Center or contact support
