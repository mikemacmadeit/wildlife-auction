# Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or need to change it, you can reset it easily.

## Forgot Password?

1. Go to the **Sign In** page
2. Click **"Forgot Password"**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

## Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Find the **"Password"** section
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (twice to confirm)
5. Click **"Update Password"**

## Password Requirements

- At least 8 characters long
- Mix of letters and numbers recommended
- Avoid common words or personal information
- Use a unique password (not used elsewhere)

## Security Tips

- Never share your password with anyone
- Use a strong, unique password
- Change your password regularly
- Enable two-factor authentication if available

## Troubleshooting

**Didn't receive reset email?**
- Check your spam folder
- Wait a few minutes
- Make sure you're using the correct email
- Contact support if needed

**Reset link expired?**
- Request a new password reset email
- Links typically expire after 24 hours
