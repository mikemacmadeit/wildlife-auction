# Setting Up Your Profile

A complete profile helps build trust with other users and enables more features.

## Required Information

- **Name**: Your full name or business name
- **Email**: Verified email address
- **Location**: City, state, and ZIP code

## Optional Information

- **Business name**: If selling as a business
- **Bio**: Brief description about yourself or your business
- **Phone number**: For direct contact (optional)
- **Profile photo**: Helps build trust

## For Sellers

Additional seller-specific information:
- **Business license** (if applicable)
- **Verification documents**
- **Payment account setup** (required to receive payouts)

## Updating Your Profile

1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update any information
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

## Profile Privacy

- Your name and location are visible to other users
- Email and phone are private unless you choose to share
- Business information is public for verified sellers

## Why Complete Your Profile?

- Builds trust with buyers and sellers
- Required for certain features (like creating listings)
- Helps with account verification
- Improves search visibility for sellers
