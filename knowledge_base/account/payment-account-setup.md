# Setting Up Payment Account (Sellers)

Sellers need to set up a payment account to receive payouts from sales.

## Why Set Up Payment Account?

- **Required to receive payouts** - Can't get paid without it
- **Secure payments** - Funds transferred directly to your account
- **Fast processing** - Receive payments quickly after sale

## Setup Process

1. **Go to Seller Dashboard** â†’ **Payment Settings**
2. **Connect payment account** - Link your bank account
3. **Verify account** - Complete verification steps
4. **Confirm setup** - Account is ready to receive payments

## Account Types Supported

- **Bank account** - Direct deposit (ACH)
- **Business account** - For business sellers
- **Other methods** - May vary by location

## Verification Steps

- **Provide account details** - Account number, routing number
- **Verify ownership** - Small test deposits may be made
- **Confirm verification** - Complete the verification process
- **Account active** - Ready to receive payouts

## Payout Timing

- **After delivery confirmation** - Payouts released after buyer confirms
- **Processing time** - 1-3 business days typically
- **Payment method** - Deposited to your connected account

## Important Notes

- **Keep account current** - Update if account details change
- **Verify before selling** - Set up before creating listings
- **Secure information** - Account details are encrypted and secure

## Troubleshooting

**Can't connect account?**
- Verify account details are correct
- Check account is active
- Contact support if issues persist

**Verification failed?**
- Double-check account information
- Ensure account is in your name
- Contact your bank if needed
